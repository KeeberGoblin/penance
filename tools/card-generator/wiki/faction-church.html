CHURCH OF ABSOLUTION

"We did not die because we were unworthy. We died because the world demanded sacrifice. Now, bound to these iron shells, we are given a second chance. Not to live, but to atone."

OVERVIEW

IDEOLOGY Redemption through violence. Faith through brutality.
CAPITAL Absolon, the Iron Cathedral-City
POPULATION ~50,000 Remnants (largest unified faction)
DOCTRINE Every kill is a prayer. Every victory brings salvation closer.
PLAYSTYLE Self-sacrifice, ally protection, aggressive martyrdom

LORE

The Church of Absolution was founded 12 years after the Sundering by the first Penitent Knights who refused to accept that death was the end. They believe that Remnants exist in a state of purgatory - neither alive nor dead - and that only through violent atonement can they earn true rest.

The Church controls the southern territories through a combination of religious fervor and military might. Their Caskets are gothic war machines covered in scripture and prayer scrolls, piloted by the dead seeking forgiveness.

CASKET TYPES

THE CONFESSOR (Light)

WEIGHT 700 lbs
SOUL POINTS 3 SP
MOVEMENT 6 hexes
ARMOR 1
DECK SIZE 25 cards

Philosophy: Fast, aggressive, self-destructive. Confessors are flagellants in iron, trading their own blood for holy fury.

Unique Mechanic: Blood Offering

  • Once per turn, mill 2 cards from your deck (self-harm)
  • If you do: Next attack deals +2d6 damage and ignores 1 armor
  • Can be used even when hand is empty (desperation move)

Faction Ability: Martyrdom

  • When reduced to 5 or fewer cards: +1 to all attack damage (permanent)
  • At 3 or fewer cards: Gain "Final Confession" (ultimate attack, 6d6 AOE, then you die)

Playstyle: Glass cannon suicide bomber. Rush in, hit hard, die gloriously.

THE MARTYR (Medium)

WEIGHT 1,100 lbs
SOUL POINTS 4 SP
MOVEMENT 4 hexes
ARMOR 2
DECK SIZE 30 cards

Philosophy: Defend the faithful. Absorb punishment meant for others.

Unique Mechanic: Take Their Burden

  • When adjacent ally takes damage, redirect it to yourself
  • Ally draws 1 card, you gain 1 "Suffering" token
  • Spend 3 Suffering: Remove 3 damage cards from discard permanently (atonement achieved)

Playstyle: Tank support. Protect team, gain power from suffering, cleanse through sacrifice.

THE CRUSADER (Heavy)

WEIGHT 1,600 lbs
SOUL POINTS 3 SP
MOVEMENT 3 hexes
ARMOR 4
DECK SIZE 35 cards

Philosophy: Wrath made manifest. The Church's hammer. Crush the heretic.

Unique Mechanic: Righteous Wrath

  • When you kill an enemy, gain 1 "Wrath" token
  • Spend Wrath: 1 for +1 movement, 2 for double damage, 3 for Holy Fury stance
  • Holy Fury: Auto-pass Morale checks, +1 damage to all attacks until end of battle

Playstyle: Momentum-based juggernaut. Kills fuel more kills. Snowball or die.

THE CATHEDRAL (Assault)

WEIGHT 2,200 lbs
SOUL POINTS 2 SP
MOVEMENT 2 hexes
ARMOR 5
DECK SIZE 40 cards

Philosophy: Mobile fortress. Walking church. When it arrives, the battlefield becomes hallowed ground.

Unique Mechanic: Consecrated Ground

  • Place "Hallowed" tokens on hexes you occupy
  • Hallowed hexes: Allies gain +1 Defense and remove 1 Taint per turn
  • Enemies: -1 to all actions, cannot summon Abominations
  • Max 6 Hallowed tokens on field

Playstyle: Area denial support. Claim territory, create safe zones, be the anchor.

ICONIC PILOT: SISTER VEX

Title: The Hollow Penitent

Casket: "The Confessional" (Heavy, 1,600 lbs)

Taint Level: 7 (Advanced Corruption)

Sister Vex believes suffering purifies. Each wound she takes brings her closer to salvation. Each ally she sacrifices is a prayer answered. The Church calls her a saint. Everyone else calls her a fanatic. She calls herself damned.

See Boss Encounter: The Iron Saint for playable boss fight.

RECOMMENDED BUILDS

THE FLAGELLANT (Confessor)

  • Dual blades, no shield
  • Maximum offense, minimum defense
  • Blood Offering every turn
  • Die by turn 5, take 3 enemies with you

THE SHIELD WALL (Martyr)

  • Tower shield, mace
  • Protect ranged allies
  • Farm Suffering tokens for permanent healing
  • Become unkillable by endgame

THE EXECUTIONER (Crusader)

  • Greatsword, heavy armor
  • Focus single targets
  • Build Wrath, enter Holy Fury
  • Sweep entire enemy team once buffed

STRATEGY NOTES

Early Game: Church starts weak. Confessors die easily. Martyrs need time to accumulate Suffering. Play defensively until turn 3.

Mid Game: Crusaders hit their stride. Once Wrath tokens start accumulating, momentum builds. Cathedral players should start claiming territory.

Late Game: Church excels in prolonged fights. Martyrs become nearly invincible. Cathedrals create impenetrable zones. Confessors use Final Confession as tactical nukes.

Weakness: High Taint vulnerability. Church pilots often have high corruption due to aggressive playstyle. Manage Taint carefully or face becoming Corrupted.
