Game Overview

Core Concept

A tactical hex-based card game where players pilot massive armored suits called Caskets in brutal medieval fantasy combat. Your deck IS your Casket - cards represent equipment, actions, and battle damage.

Core Pillars

  • Tactical Hex Combat - Positioning matters
  • Deck-Building - Your deck IS your Casket
  • Push-Your-Luck - Risk vs reward action economy
  • Corruption System - Power at a terrible price
  • Campaign Progression - Permanent consequences

Game Modes

  • Arena (PvP) - 1v1, 2v2, or FFA battles
  • Campaign (Co-op) - Story-driven progression
  • Raid (Boss Fight) - Team vs massive monsters
  • Skirmish (Mixed) - Objective-based gameplay

Deck-as-Health

Your deck represents your Casket's operational capacity. When damaged, you discard cards. When deck AND hand are empty = disabled.

Choice: Discard from hand (lose options) or mill from deck (preserve hand).

Soul-Point System

Safe Zone: Spend SP safely within your limit

Danger Zone: Push beyond for +2 SP, but roll on Strain Table (risk overheating, damage, corruption)

Corruption & Taint

Soulstones grant power but corrupt you:

  • 0-2 Taint: Clean, safe
  • 3-6 Taint: More power, more risk
  • 7-9 Taint: Forbidden abilities unlocked
  • 10 Taint: Transform into AI Abomination

üìñ Glossary of Terms

Quick reference guide to Penance terminology

Core Terms

Casket
Massive armored suit powered by a Soulstone. Your character and your deck.
Soulstone
Crystalline power core. Grants strength but corrupts your pilot over time.
Soul-Points (SP)
Action currency. Each Casket has a Safe Zone limit per turn.
Initiative
Number on cards (0-10). Lower acts first. [‚Äî] means reactive/no initiative.
Deck-as-Health
Your deck represents operational capacity. Damage = discard cards. Empty deck + hand = disabled.

Combat Terms

Safe Zone
SP you can spend per turn without risk. Varies by weight class (Scout: 5, Heavy: 3).
Danger Zone
Pushing beyond Safe Zone grants +2 SP but forces Strain Table roll.
Heat
Thermal load/system stress. High Heat increases Strain roll penalties.
Strain Table
Roll when entering Danger Zone. Higher result = worse malfunction (Heat gain, damage, forced rotation).
Facing
Hex direction you're oriented. Front attacks are normal, rear attacks devastating.

Damage Terms

Component
Casket parts: Right Arm, Left Arm, Chassis (core), Legs. Track damage separately.
Damage Card
Injury cards added to deck when reshuffling. Cannot be played, clog your hand.
Degraded
Component with 6+ damage. Penalties apply (weapons -1 dmg, shields -1 Defense, etc.).
Disabled
Component with 9+ damage (unusable) OR Casket with empty deck + hand (mission loss).
Reshuffle
When deck runs out, shuffle discard pile. Add Damage cards based on injuries (1 per 2 damage).

Corruption Terms

Taint
Corruption level (0-10). Higher Taint = more power, more risk. 10 = transform into Abomination.
Tainted Cards
Require 3+ Taint to use. Powerful effects with drawbacks.
Forbidden Cards
Require 7+ Taint. Game-breaking abilities, severe side effects.
Abomination
Player who reaches 10 Taint transforms. Becomes AI-controlled boss, hostile to all.
The Void
Dimension of chaotic magic. Source of Taint, Soulstones, and Abominations.

Card Keywords

Universal
Mandatory base cards. Every deck has exactly 10 Universal cards.
Weapon
Attack card from equipped arms (sword, hammer, bow, etc.). Requires SP.
Spell
Magic from Source Conduit arm. Choose school: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Void.
Relic
Ancient technology. Often requires ammo/charge tokens (limited uses).
Reactive
Play in response to enemy action (defense cards, interrupts). Initiative [‚Äî].
Corruption
Using this card causes Taint gain. High risk, high reward.

Campaign Terms

Workshop
Between missions, spend Credits to buy equipment, repair damage, purify Taint.
Credits
Currency earned from missions. Used at Workshop for upgrades/repairs.
Pilot Scar
Permanent trait gained every 3 missions. Can be positive, negative, or mixed.
Permadeath
Optional rule: If pilot dies in mission, gone forever. Create new pilot, lose progress.
AI Behavior Deck
Monsters act via card deck (15-20 cards). Draw each turn, execute action.

Lore Terms

The Sundering
The Cataclysm. Nikolas Theslar's experiment tore reality, killed civilization 100 years ago.
Resonance Engine
Ground zero in Sibarian Wastes. Still pulses, still tears reality. Source of Void rifts.
Penitent Knight
Human Casket pilot. Seeks absolution through combat, bound by sacred Vows.
Verdant Covenant
Elven faction. Believes world is alive, seeks harmony with Soulstones.
Forge-Guilds
Dwarven engineers. Treat Sundering as fixable problem, most advanced Soulstone research.

Range & Movement

Hex
Board space. Caskets occupy 1 hex. Has 6 facings (front, sides, rear).
Adjacent / Melee
Touching hexes. Range for swords, hammers, close combat.
Close (2-3)
2-3 hexes away. Optimal for short-range weapons (pistols, thrown).
Medium (4-6)
4-6 hexes. Standard engagement (rifles, bows, most combat).
Long (7+)
7+ hexes. Sniper range, artillery. Penalties for small targets.
Line of Sight (LOS)
Clear path to target. Terrain/obstacles can block or provide cover.

Damage & Injury System

Taking Damage

When damaged, choose how to discard:

  • From Hand: Lose tactical options now
  • From Deck: Preserve hand, risk important cards
  • Mixed: Balance both

Example: Take 4 damage with 6 cards in hand

‚Üí Discard 2 from hand + mill 2 from deck

Component Damage

Track damage per component on player mat:

  • Right Arm
  • Left Arm
  • Chassis (core)
  • Legs

9+ damage to Chassis = Destroyed

Reshuffle Injuries

When deck runs out, reshuffle and add Damage cards:

Formula: 1 Damage card per 2 damage to component

Example:

  • Right Arm: 5 damage ‚Üí 2 Damage cards
  • Chassis: 3 damage ‚Üí 1 Damage card
  • Total: 3 new Damage cards in deck

Victory & Defeat Conditions

Arena (PvP)

Victory:

  • Last Casket standing
  • Reach VP threshold (10-15 VP)
  • Most VP at time limit

Defeat: Casket disabled

Campaign (Co-op)

Victory: Complete mission objective

  • Earn credits & experience
  • Unlock equipment
  • Advance story

Defeat:

  • All Caskets disabled
  • Objective fails
  • Pilot may die (permadeath)

Raid (Boss Fight)

Victory: Reduce boss to 0 HP

Rewards:

  • Legendary equipment
  • Rare Soulstone fragments
  • Unique relic tech

Defeat: All players disabled or time expires

Skirmish (Mixed)

First to 20 VP wins:

  • Primary Objective: 10 VP
  • Secondary Objectives: 3-5 VP
  • Hidden Agendas: 2 VP
  • Eliminations: 5 VP each

Disabled State

You are disabled when:

  • Deck empty AND hand empty
  • Chassis takes 9+ damage
  • Scenario-specific condition

Co-op: You're out, but allies can continue

Special Conditions

Corruption Victory: Reach 10 Taint, transform into AI Abomination. Other players must destroy you!

Extraction: Reach zone, survive 2 turns, escape battlefield

Assembly Instructions

  1. Print card sheets on cardstock (110lb recommended) or regular paper
  2. If using regular paper, sleeve with card sleeves + backing card
