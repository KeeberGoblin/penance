Game Overview

Core Concept

A tactical hex-based card game where players pilot massive armored suits called Caskets in brutal medieval fantasy combat. Your deck IS your Casket - cards represent equipment, actions, and battle damage.

Core Pillars

  • Tactical Hex Combat - Positioning matters
  • Deck-Building - Your deck IS your Casket
  • Push-Your-Luck - Risk vs reward action economy
  • Corruption System - Power at a terrible price
  • Campaign Progression - Permanent consequences

Game Modes

  • Arena (PvP) - 1v1, 2v2, or FFA battles
  • Campaign (Co-op) - Story-driven progression
  • Raid (Boss Fight) - Team vs massive monsters
  • Skirmish (Mixed) - Objective-based gameplay

Deck-as-Health

Your deck represents your Casket's operational capacity. When damaged, you discard cards. When deck AND hand are empty = disabled.

Choice: Discard from hand (lose options) or mill from deck (preserve hand).

Soul-Point System

Safe Zone: Spend SP safely within your limit

Danger Zone: Push beyond for +2 SP, but roll on Strain Table (risk overheating, damage, corruption)

Corruption & Taint

Soulstones grant power but corrupt you:

  • 0-2 Taint: Clean, safe
  • 3-6 Taint: More power, more risk
  • 7-9 Taint: Forbidden abilities unlocked
  • 10 Taint: Transform into AI Abomination

Damage & Injury System

Taking Damage

When damaged, choose how to discard:

  • From Hand: Lose tactical options now
  • From Deck: Preserve hand, risk important cards
  • Mixed: Balance both

Example: Take 4 damage with 6 cards in hand

‚Üí Discard 2 from hand + mill 2 from deck

Component Damage

Track damage per component on player mat:

  • Right Arm
  • Left Arm
  • Chassis (core)
  • Legs

9+ damage to Chassis = Destroyed

Reshuffle Injuries

When deck runs out, reshuffle and add Damage cards:

Formula: 1 Damage card per 2 damage to component

Example:

  • Right Arm: 5 damage ‚Üí 2 Damage cards
  • Chassis: 3 damage ‚Üí 1 Damage card
  • Total: 3 new Damage cards in deck

Victory & Defeat Conditions

Arena (PvP)

Victory:

  • Last Casket standing
  • Reach VP threshold (10-15 VP)
  • Most VP at time limit

Defeat: Casket disabled

Campaign (Co-op)

Victory: Complete mission objective

  • Earn credits & experience
  • Unlock equipment
  • Advance story

Defeat:

  • All Caskets disabled
  • Objective fails
  • Pilot may die (permadeath)

Raid (Boss Fight)

Victory: Reduce boss to 0 HP

Rewards:

  • Legendary equipment
  • Rare Soulstone fragments
  • Unique relic tech

Defeat: All players disabled or time expires

Skirmish (Mixed)

First to 20 VP wins:

  • Primary Objective: 10 VP
  • Secondary Objectives: 3-5 VP
  • Hidden Agendas: 2 VP
  • Eliminations: 5 VP each

Disabled State

You are disabled when:

  • Deck empty AND hand empty
  • Chassis takes 9+ damage
  • Scenario-specific condition

Co-op: You're out, but allies can continue

Special Conditions

Corruption Victory: Reach 10 Taint, transform into AI Abomination. Other players must destroy you!

Extraction: Reach zone, survive 2 turns, escape battlefield

Assembly Instructions

  1. Print card sheets on cardstock (110lb recommended) or regular paper
  2. If using regular paper, sleeve with card sleeves + backing card
  3. Cut along card borders (sharp scissors or paper cutter)
  4. Optional: Round corners with corner rounder for professional look
  5. Sleeve all cards for consistent shuffle feel (recommended: 63.5mm x 88mm sleeves)
