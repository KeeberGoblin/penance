DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Penance follows Kingdom Death: Monster's design principles:

  • Brutal progression - Permanent consequences matter
  • Asymmetric factions - No two play the same
  • Mystery over answers - The lore raises more questions
  • Meaningful scarcity - Resources are precious
  • No pure heroes - Every faction is morally complex
