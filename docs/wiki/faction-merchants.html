Faction Overview

The Merchant Guilds control the post-apocalyptic economy. They don't fight wars—they bankroll them, sell weapons to both sides, and profit from the carnage.

"War is profitable. Peace is expensive. Choose accordingly." —Guild Ledger Principle #1
✿ ✿ ✿

Core Mechanic: Bribery & Resource Control

The Merchant Guilds wage economic warfare—buying enemies, hiring mercenaries, and controlling the battlefield through sheer wealth.

  • Bribery: Pay enemies to switch sides, bribe support units, buy temporary ceasefires
  • Hired Mercenaries: Deploy powerful units mid-battle for Credits
  • Resource Monopoly: Control Scrap nodes, deny enemies equipment, tax the battlefield
  • Economic Warfare: Win without fighting—just buy victory
✿ ✿ ✿

Playstyle (Conceptual)

Economic control. Resource denial. Hired mercenaries. Bribery and manipulation. High risk, high reward—run out of Credits and you lose everything.
