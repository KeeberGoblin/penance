Dice Pool Advantage System

Version 3.0 Optional Rules • Trench Crusade-Inspired • Replace Static Modifiers with Roll-More-Dice Drama

"Three dice. Two kept. One discarded. The difference between glory and a shallow grave."
✿

Overview

This optional system replaces static to-hit modifiers (+1, +2) with a dice pool mechanic. Instead of adding to your target number, you roll extra Attack Dice and take the highest or lowest results.

Why This Is Better:

  • More Dramatic Swings - Chance of rolling double DEATH BLOW (EXECUTION) increases dramatically
  • Feels More Exciting - Rolling more dice is viscerally fun!
  • Higher Variance at Extremes - Perfect positioning = god rolls, terrible positioning = catastrophic failures
  • Compatible - Works with existing Attack Dice symbols (STRIKE, DEATH BLOW, JAM, etc.)
✿

Core Mechanic

Base Roll: 2 Attack Dice

Standard attack (no modifiers): Roll 2 Attack Dice, add both values, compare to target number (usually 5+).

Advantage: Roll 3, Take Highest 2

When you have tactical ADVANTAGE:

  • Roll 3 Attack Dice
  • Discard the LOWEST die
  • Add the 2 highest values together

Advantage Sources: Flanking attack, higher ground, aiming, target Stunned

Example:

You're attacking from higher ground (Advantage)
Roll 3 Attack Dice: [STRIKE] (3) + [DEATH BLOW] (5) + [JAM] (0)
Discard lowest ([JAM]): Keep [STRIKE] (3) + [DEATH BLOW] (5) = 8 total
Result: Strong Hit! (+1 damage)

Critical Advantage: Roll 4, Take Highest 2

When you have MULTIPLE advantages (2+ sources):

  • Roll 4 Attack Dice
  • Discard the 2 LOWEST dice
  • Add the 2 highest values together

Why This Matters: Dramatically increases chance of double [DEATH BLOW] (EXECUTION: auto-destroy component)

Example:

Rear attack (Advantage) + Higher ground (Advantage) = Critical Advantage
Roll 4 Attack Dice: [STRIKE] (3) + [DEATH BLOW] (5) + [DEATH BLOW] (5) + [DOUBLE STRIKE] (4)
Discard 2 lowest ([STRIKE], [DOUBLE STRIKE]): Keep [DEATH BLOW] (5) + [DEATH BLOW] (5) = 10 total
Result: EXECUTION! Auto-destroy component, bypass ALL Defense!

✿

Disadvantage System

Disadvantage: Roll 3, Take Lowest 2

When you have tactical DISADVANTAGE:

  • Roll 3 Attack Dice
  • Discard the HIGHEST die
  • Add the 2 lowest values together

Disadvantage Sources: Heavy cover, long range, obscured target, high Heat (5+), attacker moved 6+ hexes

Critical Disadvantage: Roll 4, Take Lowest 2

When you have MULTIPLE disadvantages (2+ sources):

  • Roll 4 Attack Dice
  • Discard the 2 HIGHEST dice
  • Add the 2 lowest values together

Why This Hurts: Very high chance of missing or catastrophic failure (double [JAM])

✿

Advantage/Disadvantage Interactions

They CANCEL OUT (1-for-1):

  • 1 Advantage + 1 Disadvantage = Straight Roll (2 dice, no change)
  • 2 Advantages + 1 Disadvantage = Advantage (net +1, roll 3 take highest 2)
  • 1 Advantage + 2 Disadvantages = Disadvantage (net -1, roll 3 take lowest 2)

Example:

Attacking from higher ground (Advantage) through heavy cover (Disadvantage)
Net: 0 (cancel out)
Roll 2 Attack Dice normally

✿

Probability Math

Situation Chance of 7+ (Strong Hit) Chance of EXECUTION (10)
Straight Roll (2d6) ~42% ~2.8%
Advantage (3d6 take 2 highest) ~59% (+17%) ~5.1% (+2.3%)
Critical Advantage (4d6 take 2 highest) ~72% ~8.5% (TRIPLE base chance!)
Disadvantage (3d6 take 2 lowest) ~26% (-16%) ~1.2%
Critical Disadvantage (4d6 take 2 lowest) ~15% ~0.5%

This creates dramatic "god roll" moments when perfectly positioned.

Advantage/Disadvantage Sources

ADVANTAGE Sources (Roll 3, Take Highest 2)

  • Positioning: Flanking attack (hex-sides 3, 5), rear attack (hex-side 4), higher ground (1+ elevation)
  • Target Status: Target Stunned, target Immobilized, target has 5+ Momentum Tokens
  • Setup: Aiming (spent last turn stationary, aiming at target)
  • Card Effects: "Targeting Sigil" card, "Sensor Sweep" card, "Zealot's Fury" (Church Tactic)

DISADVANTAGE Sources (Roll 3, Take Lowest 2)

  • Range: Long range (7-10 hexes), extreme range (11+ hexes)
  • Cover: Heavy cover (fortress walls, dense forest), obscured target (smoke, darkness)
  • Attacker Status: Moved 6+ hexes this turn, 5+ Heat (Danger Zone), Primary Weapon destroyed
  • Target Status: Target moved 6+ hexes last turn
  • Environment: Shooting uphill (target 2+ levels higher)
