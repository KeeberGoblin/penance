THE IRON SAINT
Sister Vex, The Hollow Penitent
THREAT LEVEL: BRUTAL
✿ ✿ ✿

The Legend

Sister Vex was once a devout believer. Now she is the Church's most terrifying instrument. Taint Level 7. She pilots "The Confessional" - a 2,000lb super-heavy Casket dripping with scripture and scars, chains and blood-soaked parchment.

She believes suffering purifies. Every wound brings salvation closer. She will show you the path to redemption.

You will beg for damnation instead.

Players must balance speed (kill before Phase 4) vs safety (avoid Taint corruption). Expect casualties. Expect desperation. Expect glory.

HP Deck System Explained

Sister Vex uses a 40-card AI Behavior deck instead of traditional HP. When players damage her, they discard cards from this deck. Each card drawn determines her actions that turn. As the deck shrinks, she reshuffles and adds Phase cards—new, deadlier abilities that escalate the fight's difficulty.

How It Works:

  1. Damage = Discard: When Sister Vex takes 5 damage, discard 5 cards from her HP deck (not from hand).
  2. Boss Turn: After ALL players act, Sister Vex draws 3 cards and plays them all in order.
  3. Phase Triggers: When she reshuffles at specific card thresholds (24, 14, 7 cards), shuffle in Phase cards.
  4. Escalating Threat: Phase 1 = Basic attacks. Phase 4 = Ultimate abilities, minions, resurrection.
  5. Victory: Boss defeated when deck AND hand are empty and cannot reshuffle.

Why This Works: The boss doesn't just lose health—she transforms. Her behavior deck IS her health pool. As she takes damage and cards are removed, powerful new cards are added at phase thresholds. Early phases are manageable. Late phases are desperate survival. Players must race to kill her before Phase 4, but rushing causes mistakes. Taint accumulation punishes aggression. The fight rewards strategy, not brute force.

"The wounds open. Her Casket bleeds scripture and oil. She smiles through shattered teeth. This is where mercy dies."

Effect: Sister Vex mills 2 cards from her deck (self-damage). Attack target for 5d6 damage, ignoring 2 armor

Chains of Penance

Control

Target: Enemy with highest Taint

Effect: Target cannot move next turn. Take 3d6 damage at start of their next turn unless they discard 3 cards

"Your corruption binds you. Struggle, and tighten the noose."

DIFFICULTY SPIKE: Punishes Tainted players. Forces brutal hand discards. Mobility lockdown.

Scripture Storm

AOE Attack

"Let the holy words scour you clean."

Divine Wrath

"Strike me down? I rise. Every. Time."

DIFFICULTY SPIKE: Counterattacks punish big hits. Players must spread damage across turns.

Effect: Summon 1 "Corrupted Penitent" minion (see Minion stats below). Minion activates immediately after Sister Vex

"The faithful answer my call, even in death."

Victory & Defeat Conditions

Players Win:

Sister Vex is defeated when her deck AND hand are both empty, and she cannot reshuffle. Even if she uses "Absolution Denied" to resurrect once, she can still be defeated permanently.

Players Lose:

  • Total Party Kill: All players defeated (decks empty)
  • Corruption Victory: Any player reaches 10+ Taint and becomes permanently Corrupted (fails save)
  • Retreat: Players flee the battlefield (campaign consequence: Sister Vex hunts them)

Difficulty Variants

Easy Mode (First-Time Players):

  • Start Sister Vex with 30 cards instead of 40
  • Remove Phase 4 cards entirely
  • Minions have 6 HP instead of 10
  • No "Absolution Denied" resurrection

Hard Mode (Experienced Players):

  • Start Sister Vex with 50 cards
  • Add +1 card to each Phase expansion
  • Sister Vex draws 4 cards per turn instead of 3
  • Minions have 15 HP

Nightmare Mode (Veterans Only):

  • Start Sister Vex with 60 cards
  • Phase 2 triggers at 40 cards, Phase 3 at 25, Phase 4 at 12
  • Sister Vex draws 5 cards per turn
  • All damage from Sister Vex ignores 1 armor
  • Corrupted Penitents have 20 HP and attack twice per turn
  • "Absolution Denied" can trigger twice per battle

Setup Instructions

Boss Starting Position:

Place Sister Vex in the center of the battlefield (hex map center).

Boss Deck Construction:

Shuffle the following 40 cards to create Sister Vex's starting HP deck:

  • 10× "Zealous Strike" (Basic Attack)
  • 6× "Advance and Condemn" (Move + Attack)
  • 4× "Defensive Scripture" (Defense)
  • 5× "Martyrdom Protocol" (Regeneration)
  • 5× "Taint Pulse" (AOE Corruption)
  • 5× "Righteous Advance" (Move toward nearest enemy)
  • 5× "Shield of Faith" (Block + Counter)

Player Setup:

  • 2-4 Casket pilots (recommended 3 for balanced difficulty)
  • Standard 30-card decks (campaign decks encouraged)
  • Place players 6-8 hexes from boss (surrounding positions)
  • Each player starts with 0 Taint tokens (track on paper)

Boss Turn Sequence:

  1. After ALL players have taken their turns, Sister Vex activates
  2. Draw 3 cards from HP deck
  3. Play all 3 cards in order drawn (resolve effects)
  4. Discard cards to separate discard pile
  5. If deck is empty, reshuffle immediately and add Phase cards based on current threshold
