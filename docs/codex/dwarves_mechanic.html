The Core Mechanic: Rune Counters

Passive Generation

Generation: Gain +1 Rune Counter each time you deal damage with an attack. Complete objectives (securing zones, protecting allies) for bonus Rune Counters.

Maximum Storage: 10 Rune Counters (excess tokens are lost)

Spending: Activate powerful runic abilities, enhance attacks, or forge permanent Ancestral Runes

How It Works

Rune Counters represent the accumulated power of Dwarven craftsmanship and combat prowess. Every successful strike charges your runes, building toward devastating abilities. Here's the step-by-step:

  1. Deal Damage - Each successful attack that deals damage generates +1 Rune Counter
  2. Accumulate Power - Rune Counters persist across rounds (max 10 stored)
  3. Spend on Abilities - Use Rune Counters to activate powerful cards:
    • Runic Strike (2 Runes): +4 damage, ignore 2 Defense
    • Stone Skin (3 Runes): +3 Defense for 2 turns
    • Forge Ancestral Rune (5 Runes): Permanent +1 to damage/defense/movement
  4. Strategic Banking - Save Runes for critical moments or spend immediately for consistent power

Example Turn Sequence

Turn 1-2: Standard attacks build 2-3 Rune Counters

Turn 3: Spend 3 Runes on Stone Skin before enemy alpha strike

Turn 4-6: Continue attacking, banking Runes (now at 5-7 total)

Turn 7: Unleash Runic Strike (2 Runes) for finishing blow, leaving 5 Runes banked

The longer you survive, the more powerful you become. Dwarven patience is a weapon.

Companion Mechanic: Ancestral Runes

Ancestral Rune Forging (5 Runes): At mission end or during extended downtime, spend 5 Rune Counters to forge a permanent Ancestral Rune. Choose one benefit:

  • Rune of Power: +1 damage to all attacks permanently
  • Rune of Endurance: +1 Defense permanently
  • Rune of Swiftness: +1 movement permanently

Maximum: 3 Ancestral Runes total (mix and match as desired)

⚖️ Balance Note: Ancestral Runes persist across missions but cannot be changed once forged. Choose wisely—these represent your casket's legacy.

Strategy Guide: When to Spend Runes

✓ Good Times to Spend Runes

  • Before Taking Heavy Fire - Stone Skin (3 Runes) turns you into an unkillable fortress
  • Executing High-Value Targets - Runic Strike (2 Runes) guarantees kills on armored enemies
  • Late Campaign Missions - Banking 5 Runes to forge Ancestral Runes provides permanent scaling
  • Breaking Stalemates - Spending 4-5 Runes on consecutive powerful turns can shatter defensive lines
  • Protecting Allies - Runic Ward (3 Runes) grants adjacent allies +2 Defense, perfect for support builds

✗ Bad Times to Spend Runes

  • Early Turns (1-3) - You haven't banked enough Runes yet. Be patient, build your reserves
  • Against Weak Enemies - Don't waste Runes overkilling Thralls. Save for real threats
  • When Already Winning - If you're dominating, bank Runes for next mission or Ancestral Rune forging
  • Without a Plan - Spending 2 Runes randomly is wasteful. Have a specific goal (survive, execute, etc.)
  • At Max Runes (10) - If you're capped, you're wasting generation. Spend to make room

Advanced Tactics & Combos

The Stone Fortress

Defensive Combo:

Stone Skin (3 Runes, +3 Defense for 2 turns) → Tower Shield Stance (+2 Defense, block adjacent hex) → Reinforced Plating (+1 Defense armor)

Result: 7-8 Defense total for 2 turns. Most factions deal 3-5 damage per attack. You're taking 0-1 damage per hit while counterattacking.

The Rune-Charged Assault

Aggressive Combo:

Bank 8 Runes → Runic Strike (2 Runes, +4 damage, ignore 2 Defense) → Warhammer Overhead Smash (5 SP, 7 damage base) → Runic Strike again (2 Runes)

Total: Two attacks dealing 11 damage each (7 base + 4 bonus), ignoring 2 Defense. 22 damage total, enough to kill most caskets.

The Ancestral Legacy Builder

Long-Term Strategy:

Mission 1-3: Forge Rune of Power (+1 damage)
Mission 4-6: Forge Rune of Endurance (+1 Defense)
Mission 7+: Forge Rune of Swiftness (+1 movement)

By late campaign, you have +1/+1/+1 to damage/defense/movement permanently. You're a veteran warrior with compounding advantages.

The Shield Wall Synergy

Team Combo:

Runic Ward (3 Runes, adjacent allies gain +2 Defense) → Position with 2-3 allies in line → All allies gain +2 Defense while you hold the line with Tower Shield

You become the anchor of your team's defensive line, making everyone harder to kill while generating more Runes from counterattacks.

Common Mistakes

❌ Mistake #1: Hoarding Runes Without Spending

Why it fails: Sitting at 10/10 Runes means you're wasting generation. Each attack that would generate a Rune is lost value.

Fix: Spend down to 5-7 Runes periodically. Use Stone Skin or Runic Strikes to cycle your resource pool.

❌ Mistake #2: Spending All Runes Too Early

Why it fails: Using all 4 Runes on turn 3 leaves you with no resources for critical moments (turns 6-8).

Fix: Maintain a reserve of 3-5 Runes. Only dip below 3 in emergencies or when forging Ancestral Runes.

❌ Mistake #3: Ignoring Ancestral Rune Forging

Why it fails: Ancestral Runes are permanent upgrades that compound across missions. Ignoring them means you plateau in power.

Fix: Prioritize banking 5 Runes at mission end. Forge at least 1 Ancestral Rune by Mission 5.

❌ Mistake #4: Playing Too Passively

Why it fails: Rune Counters are generated by dealing damage. If you're turtling with Tower Shield without attacking, you're not building resources.

Fix: Balance defense with aggression. Attack every turn (even chip damage) to keep Runes flowing.

Matchup-Specific Tips

vs. Church of Absolution (Burst Damage)

Church relies on Blood Offering alpha strikes. Save 3 Runes for Stone Skin on Turn 1-2 to negate their opening. Your Defense scaling counters their glass cannon aggression.

vs. The Ossuarium (Resurrection)

Ossuarium revives constantly, giving you free Rune generation. Each kill generates a Rune even if they resurrect. Farm their Bone Thralls for 5+ Runes, then use Runic Strikes to permanently execute key targets.

vs. Elven Verdant Covenant (Bleed Stacking)

Elves stack Bleed over time. Your Stone Skin (3 Runes) and Reinforced Plating negate Bleed damage. Runic Ward protects allies from Bleed stacks. Ignore their mobility—focus on defense until they run out of cards.

vs. Vestige Bloodlines (Mutation Burst)

Bloodlines snowball if unchecked. Use Runic Strikes early (turns 2-3) to kill them before mutations spiral. Don't let them reach 5+ Biomass tokens—your armor means nothing against 15-damage mutations.

vs. The Wyrd Conclave (Reality Manipulation)

Wyrd mechanics steal and manipulate. Your Rune Counters are YOUR resource—they can't steal them with Stolen Face. Bank Runes safely, then unleash when you have lethal. Consistency beats trickery.
