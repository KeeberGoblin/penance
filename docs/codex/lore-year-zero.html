Year 0: The First Days

"I remember when the sky turned red. When the ground shook for three days straight. When the world ended and didn't have the decency to kill us all at once." -Elder Miriam, Nomad Collective

Hour-by-hour, day-by-day account of civilization's collapse. From detonation to faction formation.

✿

Day 0: The Detonation

Hour 0 (14:32 UTC)

Time Event
14:32:00 ENGINE BREACH: Arctic ice shelf vaporizes. 182 souls consumed. Void opens
14:32:03 Electromagnetic pulse expands at light speed. Global electronics fail
14:32:45 Magnitude 9.4 earthquake. 214 volcanoes erupt simultaneously
14:45 Crimson Aurora manifests globally. Witnesses report "the sky screaming"

Hours 1-6 (15:00-20:00 UTC)

  • 15:30 - First Void incursion (Arctic research stations attacked)
  • 16:00 - Global power grid cascade failure (94% blackout)
  • 17:00 - Ashveil Caldera erupts (80,000 Cinder Tribes killed, 15,000 goblinoids survive)
  • 18:00 - 200-foot tsunami hits Alaska (45,000 dead)
  • 20:00 - Ash cloud reaches stratosphere (Ashfall Winter begins)

Hours 7-24

  • 22:00 - Mass riots, looting, fires in cities worldwide
  • Midnight - Last functioning radio relay stations go dark. Radio networks completely offline
  • 02:00 - Hospital failures (life support dies, 200,000 dead)
  • 06:00 (Dawn, Day 1) - Survivors emerge. Highways gridlocked with abandoned cars

Casualties Day 0: 1.2 billion dead

✿

Days 1-3: The Die-Off

Day 1 - Exposure Deaths

  • Morning: Global temperature drops 5°C (ash blocking sun). 800,000 freeze to death
  • Afternoon: Clean water scarcity critical. Dysentery outbreaks
  • Evening: First Void storms detected. Crimson lightning strikes

Casualties Day 1: 1.8 billion (cumulative: 3.0 billion)

Day 2 - Violence Erupts

  • Grocery stores emptied by looters
  • Armed confrontations over food/water
  • Police/military unable to maintain order
  • Void-corrupted animals appear in cities

Casualties Day 2: 1.2 billion (cumulative: 4.2 billion)

Day 3 - Infrastructure Collapse

  • Uncontrolled fires spread (major cities 40-60% burned)
  • Chemical plants explode
  • 14 nuclear reactors begin meltdowns
  • "Shamblers" spotted (Void-mutated humans)

Casualties Day 3: 900 million (cumulative: 5.1 billion)

"By Day 3, we understood: This wasn't temporary. This was the end. The world we knew was gone, and we were living in its corpse."
✿

Days 4-7: Finding Shelter

Survivor Strategies

Strategy Description Survival Rate
Underground Subway tunnels, basements, bunkers Highest (safest from Void/violence)
Rural Farms, isolated cabins High (food access, self-sufficient)
Fortified Barricaded buildings Medium (temporary safety)
Urban Remained in cities 5% (density, no infrastructure)

Day 6 - The First Soul-Binding

Location: Collapsed research facility, former Oregon
14:00 - Dr. Marcus Thresher binds deceased wife's soul to industrial exoskeleton
Result: First Casket activated. Founding moment of the Ossuarium

Casualties Day 4-7: 1.6 billion (cumulative: 6.7 billion)
89% of humanity dead in first week

✿

Week 2-4: Stabilization

Week 2 (Day 8-14)

  • Scavenging Phase: Survivors find clean water, food stores, weapons
  • Void Activity Increases: Abominations appear daily
  • Proto-Factions Form: Religious survivors (future Church), mining communities (future Dwarves), refugee caravans (future Nomads)

Week 3 (Day 15-21)

  • Warlord Era Begins: Strongest survivors claim territory
  • Resource Wars: Battles over fuel, food, weapons, medicine
  • Bonelord Thresh's Expansion: Binds 23 more corpses. First Casket platoon (24 units)

Week 4 (Day 22-30)

  • Ashfall Winter Intensifies: Temperature now -10°C below pre-Sundering
  • Agricultural Collapse: 95% of crops fail
  • Tribal Organization: Groups of 10-500 form for survival

Survivor Count (Day 30): ~750 million

✿

Months 2-12: The Long Winter

Month 2 - Starvation

Scavenged food running out. No farms (ash covers fields, sunlight blocked). Hunting difficult. Desperate measures: cannibalism widespread, especially in cities.

Months 3-5 - Adaptation

  • Fire Worship: Crucible Packs (goblinoids) survive in volcanic lava tubes
  • Underground Living: Proto-Dwarves thrive in deep shelters
  • Nomadic Lifestyle: Proto-Nomads keep moving, scavenge new areas
  • Faith: Proto-Church provides hope and social cohesion

Day 90 - Bonelord Thresh's Ossuarium Founded
First "Soul Harvesting" facility established. Offers deal: "Sign contract, your family eats. You die, your soul powers Casket." 1,200 desperate survivors sign (first voluntary contracts).

Month 7-8 - The Thaw

Volcanic ash begins settling from atmosphere. Sunlight increases (still dim). Temperature rises slightly (-5°C below pre-Sundering). First crops planted (80% fail, 20% succeed).

Months 9-10 - Faction Coalescence

Faction Leader Members (Day 287)
Church of Absolution Grand Inquisitor Aldric 12,000
Forge-Guilds Forgemaster Durak 8,000
The Ossuarium Bonelord Thresh 3,500 Caskets + 15,000 contract-holders
Nomad Collective Warlord Kess 20,000

Day 287 - First Inter-Faction Trade: Dwarves trade tools for Church food. Nomads trade scavenged fuel for Ossuarium protection. Economy reborn (barter, not currency).
