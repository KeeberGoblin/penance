Why Didn't Humanity Recover?

With 680 million survivors at Year 1, why didn't populations rebound? Six compounding factors created an extinction spiral:

1. The Fertility Crisis

  • Void Radiation Exposure: 40% of survivors rendered sterile or producing non-viable offspring within first 20 years
  • Genetic Bottleneck: Small surviving populations led to inbreeding, elevated infant mortality (60% died before age 5 in Years 1-50)
  • Maternal Mortality: Without hospitals, 1 in 8 mothers died in childbirth (vs. 1 in 5,000 pre-Sundering)
  • Average Fertility Rate: 1.2-1.8 children per woman (below replacement rate of 2.1)

2. Environmental Collapse

  • Ashfall Winter: Lasted 15 years, making agriculture unreliable until Year 15
  • Soil Contamination: Ash mixed with Void radiation made 60% of arable land toxic
  • Water Scarcity: Most groundwater contaminated; clean water rare and contested
  • Climate Instability: Temperature swings of ±20°C unpredictably; crop failures every 3-5 years

3. Disease and Medical Collapse

  • Void Plague (Year 10): 15% mortality across all factions; no cure found
  • Second Void Plague (Year 287): 22% mortality; hit hardest among children
  • Loss of Modern Medicine: Antibiotics expired by Year 5; surgery without anesthesia; no vaccines
  • New Diseases: Void-mutated pathogens with novel transmission vectors
  • Average Lifespan: Dropped from 78 years (pre-Sundering) to 42 years (Year 100) to 38 years (current)

4. Perpetual Warfare

  • Great Faction Wars (Years 15-30): Church vs Ossuarium, Dwarves vs Crucible—killed 80 million
  • Resource Wars: Fighting over clean water, arable land, Soulstone deposits
  • Void Incursions: Constant attacks from Void abominations; settlements wiped out regularly
  • Raiding Culture: Nomads, bandits, desperate factions preying on weaker settlements

5. Loss of Knowledge and Technology

  • First Generation Die-Off (Years 0-50): Pre-Sundering doctors, engineers, scientists died without passing on knowledge
  • No Industrial Base: Cannot manufacture antibiotics, fertilizers, complex machinery
  • Information Loss: Digital records gone; paper records burned; oral tradition unreliable
  • Dependency on Magic: Factions use Soulstone magic as crutch, not sustainable agriculture/engineering

6. Psychological and Cultural Breakdown

  • Trauma and PTSD: First 3 generations grew up in constant violence; mental illness rampant
  • Loss of Hope: Many survivors refused to have children ("Why bring life into this hell?")
  • Faction Isolationism: Distrust prevents cooperation, genetic mixing, shared knowledge
  • Fatalism: Belief that humanity is meant to die out; some religions actively oppose childbirth

The Mathematical Reality

Current Trajectory: If birth rates remain below replacement (1.2-1.8 vs. 2.1 needed), humanity will be extinct within 800-1,200 years.

Extinction Acceleration Factors:

  • Genetic bottleneck worsening (only 9.2M to draw from)
  • Ongoing Void expansion consuming habitable land
  • Climate instability preventing agricultural stability
  • Faction wars depleting young male populations
  • Soulstone dependency (using souls for survival = fewer potential parents)

What Could Reverse It:

  • Inter-faction peace and genetic mixing
  • Discovery of Void purification method (reclaim contaminated land)
  • Medical renaissance (reduce infant/maternal mortality)
  • Successful agricultural magic (stable food = population growth)
  • Cultural shift toward valuing childbirth and family
