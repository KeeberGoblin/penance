Balance Proposal: A-Tier Parity

October 19, 2025 - Bringing all 10 factions to competitive A-B tier status

Executive Summary

Goal: Balance all 10 factions to 45-55% win rate (±5% variance acceptable)

Current State: S-tier (Elves), A-tier (Ossuarium, Dwarves), B-tier (Church), C-tier (Exchange, Nomads, Crucible), D-tier (Wyrd, Bloodlines, Emergent)

Target State: All factions A-tier competitive

Test Plan: 120-200 game simulation campaign over 4 weeks

Phase 1: S/A-Tier Nerfs (Week 1)

Elven Verdant Covenant (S → A)

Problem: TTK 5.0 turns (too fast), regenerates 36% HP per game (3 cards × 12 turns)

Changes:

  • Bleed cap: 10 → 8 stacks (prevents turn 9+ runaway scaling)
  • Leaf Dance: 3 hexes → 2 hexes (Move 3 + Attack + Move 3 → Move 2 + Attack + Move 2)
  • Remove Photosynthesis (passive 1 card/round regen), keep Verdant Regeneration (2 cards/round active)
  • Arcing Shot damage: 3d6 → 2d6 (AoE damage reduction)
  • Thorn Armor: Reflect 2 → Reflect 1 (defensive scaling)

Math: Bleed damage turn 8 = 8 stacks × 2 damage = 16 damage (vs 20 at cap 10). TTK 5.0 → 6.0 turns (+20%). Regen 36% → 24% (-36% survivability).

Playstyle: Still mobile assassin with Bleed stacking, but capped earlier and less regeneration.

The Ossuarium (A → A, Less Oppressive)

Problem: 3 resurrection mechanics + 100% lifesteal = impossible to kill

Changes:

  • Phylactery resurrection: 5 HP → 3 HP (one less turn to re-kill)
  • Soul Harvest lifesteal: 100% → 50% (4 damage = 2 cards back, not 4)
  • Remove "Phylactery Relic" equipment (no second Phylactery)
  • Corpse Fuel: 5 cards → 4 cards (on kill recovery)

Math: Soul Harvest efficiency 100% → 50% (-50% TTK). Phylactery 5 HP → 3 HP (1 less turn). Total survivability -20%.

Playstyle: Still vampire lifesteal, but kill-able with focused fire.

Dwarven Forge-Guilds (A → A, Faster TTK)

Problem: 32 HP + Rune -3 = takes 11.4 turns to kill (too tanky)

Changes:

  • Base HP: 32 → 30 (same as all factions)
  • Rune of Protection cap: -3 → -2 damage (max 2 stacks)
  • Clan Vengeance: +2 → +1 damage per Component Damage
  • Armor-Piercing: All attacks → Rune-enhanced attacks only (must have 1+ Rune counter)
  • BUFF: +1 base damage on all attacks (to compensate for armor-piercing nerf)

Math: Effective HP with Runes: 42 → 34 (-19%). TTK 11.4 → 6.6 turns (-42%). Offensive output +15% (base damage buff).

Playstyle: Still tanky defender, but killable. Faster kills offset by less survivability.

Phase 2: C-Tier Buffs (Week 2)

The Exchange (C → A)

Problem: War Profiteer heals 1 HP/5 damage (20% efficiency) = 6 HP total heal. Too weak.

Changes:

  • War Profiteer (The Ledger): 1 HP per 5 damage → 1 HP per 3 damage (33% healing, +50%)
  • Compounding Interest: +1 damage/turn → +2 damage/turn (vs same target, ×2 scaling)
  • Soul Drain count: ×5 → ×7 in deck (more consistency)
  • Contract Breach: 3d6 → 4d6 damage (burst capability)

Math: Healing 6 HP → 10 HP (+67%). Compounding Interest turn 5 = +10 damage (vs +5). TTK 8.8 → 4.3 turns (-51%).

Playstyle: Economic warfare with healing sustain and focus-fire scaling.

Nomad Collective (C → A)

Problem: 7 Move but no defensive/sustain mechanics = dies too fast

Changes:

  • Keep 7 Move (fastest faction identity)
  • Ghost Step: 1 token → 3 tokens per mission (teleport 4 hexes, ignore terrain/LOS)
  • Opportunist: +1 damage → +3 damage (vs enemies with 1+ Damage cards)
  • Scrapper: 1 Scrap → 3 Scrap on Component Damage (economy boost)
  • NEW: "Evasive Maneuvers" - Once per turn, move 2 hexes after being attacked (×3 in deck)

Math: Ghost Step 3× = 3 free repositions per game. Opportunist +3 = 50% damage boost (mid/late). Survivability +14% (Evasive Maneuvers). TTK 6.0 → 5.2 turns (-13%).

Playstyle: Ultra-mobile guerrilla fighter, hardest faction to pin down.

Crucible Pacts (C → A)

Problem: Requires taking damage to activate mechanics (Blood Rage), too risky

Changes:

  • Living Forge: 1 Forge token/turn → 2 Forge tokens/turn (faster economy)
  • Blood Rage: +1 damage per 5 HP lost → +2 damage per 5 HP lost (×2 scaling)
  • Volcanic Terrain: 1d6 damage → 2d6 damage (environment hazard)
  • Honor Duel: +2 damage → +4 damage (when 1v1, no allies within 5 hexes)
  • BUFF: 30 HP → 32 HP base (compensates for Blood Rage self-harm)

Math: Blood Rage at 15 HP lost = +6 damage (vs +3). Living Forge turn 5 = 10 tokens (vs 5). TTK 7.2 → 4.2 turns (-42%). Survivability 30 → 32 HP (+6.7%).

Playstyle: Fire duelist who gets stronger when wounded, volcanic battlefield control.

Phase 3: D-Tier Buffs (Week 3)

The Wyrd Conclave (D → A)

Problem: Untested, complex mechanics, likely underpowered

Changes:

  • Bargain mechanic: 1 per mission → 2 per turn (steal abilities: 2 SP + discard 1 card)
  • Between Moments: 1 Wyrd token → 3 Wyrd tokens (stored for later phases)
  • Reality Bending: 2d6 → 3d6 damage (ignores Defense, costs 2 Wyrd tokens)
  • Stolen Faces: +1 Defense → +2 Defense (when using stolen ability)
  • NEW: "Illusory Double" - Create decoy at your location, becomes real if attacked (×2 in deck, costs 1 Wyrd token)

Math: Bargain 2/turn = steal 24 abilities per game (action economy advantage). Reality Bending 3d6 = 10.5 avg damage (ignores defense). Survivability +31% (Stolen Faces +2 Defense, Illusory Double). TTK 7.0 → 3.7 turns (-47%).

Playstyle: Reality-warping trickster, steals enemy abilities, creates illusions.

Vestige Bloodlines (D → A)

Problem: Extinction Clock (suicide timer) makes faction unplayable long-term

Changes:

  • Biomass generation: 1/turn → 2/turn (faster mutations)
  • Pack Tactics (Wolf Form): +1 damage → +2 damage per ally
  • Titan's Endurance (Bear Form): +5 HP → +8 HP
  • Adaptive Mutations: 2 forms → 5 forms available (Wolf, Bear, Serpent, Bat, Mantis)
  • REMOVE: Extinction Clock (no more suicide timer - faction is now sustainable)

Math: Biomass 2/turn = 24 biomass total (12 turns). Pack Tactics with 2 allies = +4 damage. Titan's Endurance +8 HP. Removal of Extinction Clock = infinite survivability. TTK 8.2 → 6.2 turns (-25%). Survivability +52% (no suicide timer).

Playstyle: Mutation specialists with pack tactics, sustainable evolution.

Emergent Syndicate (D → A)

Problem: Untested swarm mechanics, likely weak individual units

Changes:

  • Metamorph generation: 1/turn → 2/turn (faster form changes)
  • Assault Form: 2d6 damage → 3d6 damage
  • Tank Form: +2 Defense → +3 Defense
  • Hive-Mind Coordination: +1 accuracy → +2 accuracy per ally (within 3 hexes)
  • Swarm Tactics: 1d6 damage → 2d6 damage (when 2+ allies attack same target)

Math: Metamorph 2/turn = 24 total (form flexibility). Assault Form 3d6 = 10.5 avg damage. Hive-Mind with 2 allies = +4 accuracy (near auto-hit). TTK 7.2 → 4.8 turns (-34%). Survivability +9% (Tank Form +3 Defense).

Playstyle: Form-shifting hive-mind with coordinated swarm tactics.

Church of Absolution (B-Tier Tweaks)

Status: Recently buffed (Blood Offering 2 HP → 1 HP), now B-tier viable

Minor Adjustments:

  • Righteous Fury: Infinite scaling → Cap at +3 damage (prevents infinite scaling, still strong)
  • BUFF: 30 HP → 32 HP base (compensates for self-harm, matches Dwarves/Crucible)

Math: Righteous Fury turn 10 = +3 damage (vs potentially +10 infinite). HP 30 → 32 (+6.7%). Win rate should remain 45-55%.

Playstyle: Aggressive martyrdom with capped scaling (prevents late-game runaway).

Test Plan (4-Week Campaign)

Week 1: S/A-Tier Nerfs

  • Apply Elves, Ossuarium, Dwarves nerfs
  • 30 games: Each S-tier faction vs Church (10 games each)
  • Success Criteria: 40-60% win rate for all S-tier factions
  • Rollback Trigger: If win rate <35% or >65%, revert 50% of nerfs

Week 2: C-Tier Buffs

  • Apply Exchange, Nomads, Crucible buffs
  • 30 games: Each C-tier faction vs Church (10 games each)
  • Success Criteria: 40-60% win rate for all C-tier factions
  • Rollback Trigger: If win rate <35%, apply +25% additional buffs

Week 3: D-Tier Buffs

  • Apply Wyrd, Bloodlines, Emergent buffs
  • 30 games: Each D-tier faction vs Church (10 games each)
  • Success Criteria: 40-60% win rate for all D-tier factions
  • Rollback Trigger: If win rate <35%, redesign faction mechanics

Week 4: Cross-Faction Testing

  • 90 games: Round-robin tournament (10 factions × 9 matchups)
  • Success Criteria: All factions 40-60% overall win rate, no matchup >60%
  • Hotfix Protocol: Apply ±10% adjustments to outliers

Implementation Priority

  1. Critical (Apply Immediately): Elves Bleed cap 8, Ossuarium Soul Harvest 50%, Dwarves 30 HP
  2. High (Week 1): Exchange War Profiteer buff, Nomads Ghost Step 3×, Crucible Living Forge ×2
  3. Medium (Week 2): Wyrd Bargain 2/turn, Bloodlines remove Extinction Clock, Emergent Metamorph 2/turn
  4. Low (Week 3): Church Righteous Fury cap +3, minor numerical tweaks
