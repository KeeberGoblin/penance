Mission Generation Tables

Procedural Encounters for Campaign & Random Missions

"Every mission is a gamble. Roll the dice, deploy your Casket, and pray the odds favor you."
✿

Overview: When to Generate Missions

Use these tables to generate enemy compositions for:

  • Random Campaign Missions - Between story missions, generate side quests
  • Settlement Defense Events - When Settlement Defense triggers (see Settlement Phase)
  • Random Encounters - Travel encounters, wilderness patrols, scavenging runs
  • Custom Scenarios - Build your own missions with balanced enemy groups
✿

Step 1: Determine Mission Difficulty

Roll 1d6 or choose based on player strength:

1d6 Difficulty Enemy Budget Description
1-2 Easy 30-40 HP total Intro mission. Mostly Swarm (3-5 HP) enemies. 1 Elite max.
3-4 Medium 50-70 HP total Standard mission. Mix of Swarm and Elite (15-25 HP) enemies.
5 Hard 80-100 HP total Challenging. 2-3 Elites + Swarm support, or 1 Boss (30-60 HP) + adds.
6 Deadly 120+ HP total Campaign climax. 1 Boss + 2 Elites + Swarm, or Anomalous entity.
✿

Step 2: Determine Enemy Faction

Roll 1d12 to determine which faction you're fighting:

1d12 Faction Tactical Style
1 Church of Absolution Self-harm for burst damage. Martyrdom. Aggressive.
2 Dwarven Forge-Guilds Rune stacking. Tank. Slow but unkillable.
3 Elven Verdant Covenant Bleed stacking. Hit-and-run. DoT specialists.
4 The Exchange Resource drain. Economic warfare. Anti-healing.
5 Nomad Collective Extreme mobility. Scavengers. Hit-and-fade.
6 Blighted Packs Taint damage. Disease. Suicide bombers.
7 Crucible Pacts Escalating damage when wounded. Berserkers.
8 Tainted Creatures Corrupted wildlife. Swarm tactics. Taint infliction.
9 Bandits/Raiders Human enemies. Standard tactics. Salvage focus.
10 Rogue Caskets Rival pilots. Mirror player abilities. Tactical.
11 Automata/Constructs Scrap Golems, Drones. Immune to Taint. Predictable.
12 Anomalous Roll on Anomalous Entity table (see Anomalous Bestiary).
✿

Step 3: Generate Enemy Composition

Based on difficulty and faction, roll on the appropriate table:

EASY Missions (30-40 HP Budget)

1d6 Composition Total HP
1-2 10× Swarm (3 HP each) 30 HP
3-4 8× Swarm (3-5 HP) + 1× Elite (15 HP) ~35 HP
5-6 6× Swarm (5 HP each) 30 HP
Example (Church, Easy, Roll 3):
- 8× Tainted Rats (3 HP each) = 24 HP
- 1× Church Zealot (18 HP) = 18 HP
Total: 42 HP - Slightly over budget but manageable for 2-3 players.

MEDIUM Missions (50-70 HP Budget)

1d6 Composition Total HP
1-2 6× Swarm + 2× Elite ~50 HP
3-4 10× Swarm + 1× Elite ~60 HP
5-6 3× Elite (faction-specific) ~60 HP
Example (Nomads, Medium, Roll 5):
- 3× Nomad Outrider (15 HP each) = 45 HP
Total: 45 HP - Three highly mobile enemies. Extreme hit-and-run tactics. Chase nightmare.

HARD Missions (80-100 HP Budget)

1d6 Composition Total HP
1-2 1× Boss + 6× Swarm ~60 HP
3-4 2× Elite + 8× Swarm ~70 HP
5-6 1× Boss + 2× Elite ~80 HP
Example (Blighted, Hard, Roll 6):
- 1× Tainted Leviathan (60 HP Boss) = 60 HP
- 2× Blighted Plague Bearer (18 HP each) = 36 HP
Total: 96 HP - Boss with two suicide bombers. AoE Taint nightmare. Keep distance or die.

DEADLY Missions (120+ HP Budget)

1d6 Composition Total HP
1-2 1× Boss + 3× Elite + 6× Swarm ~120 HP
3-4 2× Boss + 10× Swarm ~140 HP
5 4× Elite + 12× Swarm ~120 HP
6 1× Anomalous Entity (Damnatio/Absolutio class) Special
Example (Anomalous, Deadly, Roll 6):
- 1× The Void Shepherd (60 HP Damnatio-class) = 60 HP
Total: 60 HP - But HP is misleading. Reality-eroding aura, dimension-stepping, cannot be permanently killed. This is a SURVIVAL mission, not a kill mission.
✿

Step 4: Add Mission Modifiers (Optional)

Roll 1d6. On a 6, add a modifier:

1d6 Modifier Effect
1 Reinforcements Enemies spawn 1d3 additional Swarm units on Turn 3, 6, 9.
2 Elite Commander All enemies gain +1 to hit while Commander alive. Kill Commander first.
3 Hazardous Terrain Taint zones, lava, Void rifts. Movement restricted. Environmental damage.
4 Ambush Enemies deploy first. Surprise round (enemies act, players don't).
5 Mixed Faction Enemies from 2 different factions (roll twice on Faction table).
6 Special Objective Can't just kill enemies. Must rescue civilian, hold position, or destroy objective.
✿

Quick Reference: Mission Generation Flowchart

  1. Roll 1d6 → Determine Difficulty (Easy/Medium/Hard/Deadly)
  2. Roll 1d12 → Determine Faction
  3. Roll 1d6 on Composition Table for that Difficulty
  4. Select enemies from Faction Pool to match HP budget
  5. Roll 1d6 → On a 6, add Mission Modifier
  6. Deploy!
