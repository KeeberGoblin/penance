The Core Mechanic: Soul Shard Salvage

Passive Resource Generation

Generation: Each time an enemy is killed, collect 1 Soul Shard (2 Shards if kill was made with a Tainted card)

Maximum Storage: 15 Soul Shards (excess Shards are lost)

Spending Options:

  • Repair (3 Shards): Recover 3 cards from discard
  • Draw Power (2 Shards): Draw 2 cards immediately
  • Necrotic Surge (4 Shards): +3 damage to all attacks this turn
  • Raise Thrall (5 Shards): Summon a Bone Thrall ally (8 HP, 3 damage)

⚖️ Balance Update Oct 19 (v6.0): Doubled Shard generation (+1 per kill baseline, +2 from Tainted kills). Reduced Raise Thrall cost from 7 to 5 Shards.

How It Works

Soul Shard Salvage is The Ossuarium's economy system—every kill becomes a resource. The faction's playstyle revolves around attrition: outlast your enemies, salvage their souls, use Shards to repair/buff/summon. Here's the cycle:

  1. Kill Enemies - Each kill generates 1 Soul Shard (2 if using Tainted cards)
  2. Bank Shards - Shards persist across rounds, up to 15 maximum
  3. Spend Strategically - Use Shards to:
    • Repair damage (3 Shards = 3 cards recovered)
    • Draw more options (2 Shards = 2 cards drawn)
    • Burst damage (4 Shards = +3 damage to all attacks)
    • Summon allies (5 Shards = Bone Thrall)
  4. Grinder Loop - Use Bone Thralls to generate more kills → more Shards → more Thralls. Snowball through attrition.

Example Mission Arc

Turns 1-3: Kill 2-3 enemies using regular attacks (3 Shards collected)

Turn 4: Spend 3 Shards on Repair (recover from damage taken)

Turns 5-7: Continue grinding enemies (now at 6-8 Shards)

Turn 8: Spend 5 Shards to Raise Thrall (extra body to absorb hits)

Turns 9+: Thrall generates kills → more Shards → Raise second Thrall

The longer the mission, the more overwhelming you become. You ARE the attrition.

Companion Mechanic: Taint Corruption

Taint Accumulation (Passive): Each time you use a Tainted card, gain +1 Taint permanently. Taint provides benefits but accumulates risk:

  • At 1-3 Taint: Kills with Tainted cards generate +1 Soul Shard (2 total)
  • At 4-6 Taint: Tainted cards gain +1 damage
  • At 7-9 Taint: Tainted cards gain +2 damage but you take +1 damage from all attacks
  • At 10+ Taint: Permanent corruption. You're Void-touched but incredibly powerful

Taint is PERMANENT - Cannot be removed, only managed. High-risk, high-reward scaling.

Strategy Guide: When to Spend Shards

✓ Good Times to Spend Shards

  • After Taking Heavy Damage - Repair (3 Shards) is your healing. Use liberally when below 15 HP
  • Before Boss Fights - Raise Thrall (5 Shards) gives you an extra body to tank damage
  • When Hand is Weak - Draw Power (2 Shards) cycles your deck for better cards
  • For Burst Finishing Moves - Necrotic Surge (4 Shards) + multiple attacks can wipe entire enemy teams
  • Mid-Late Game (Turn 6+) - By then you have 8-12 Shards. Spend aggressively to snowball

✗ Bad Times to Spend Shards

  • Early Game (Turns 1-3) - You've only collected 2-4 Shards. Be patient, build reserves
  • When Already Winning - If you're dominating and at full HP, bank Shards for next mission
  • Spending All Shards at Once - Maintain 3-5 Shard reserve for emergencies. Don't zero yourself out
  • Raising Thralls Without Targets - Don't summon Thralls when there are no enemies to kill. They're fragile and waste Shards if unused
  • Repair When at Full HP - Don't waste 3 Shards on Repair if you haven't taken damage

Advanced Tactics & Combos

The Grinder Loop

Snowball Combo:

Raise Thrall (5 Shards) → Thrall kills enemy (+1 Shard) → Collect Shards from your kills → Raise second Thrall (5 Shards) → Thralls generate more kills → Raise third Thrall

Result: By turn 10, you have 3 Bone Thralls tanking damage and generating Shards. You become unkillable through numbers.

The Taint Spike

Aggressive Combo:

Accumulate 4-6 Taint (Tainted cards gain +1 damage) → Necrotic Surge (4 Shards, +3 damage) → Tainted Cleave (5 base damage + 1 Taint + 3 Surge = 9 damage AoE)

Total: 9 damage to multiple targets, generates 2 Shards per kill (Tainted bonus). You recover the Shard cost immediately.

The Infinite Sustain

Attrition Combo:

Take damage (down to 10 HP) → Repair (3 Shards, recover 3 cards) → Continue fighting → Kill 2-3 enemies (+3 Shards) → Repair again (3 Shards)

Net result: As long as you're killing enemies, you can Repair indefinitely. You outlast everyone through economic advantage.

The Void Gambit

High-Risk Combo:

Intentionally accumulate 7-9 Taint (Tainted cards +2 damage, you take +1 damage) → Spam Tainted attacks for massive burst → Each kill generates 2 Shards → Use Shards to Repair constantly

You're a glass cannon who heals through killing. High Taint = high damage = more kills = more Shards = more healing. Dangerous but devastating.

Common Mistakes

❌ Mistake #1: Hoarding Shards Without Spending

Why it fails: Sitting at 15/15 Shards means you're wasting generation. Every kill beyond cap is lost value.

Fix: Spend down to 10 Shards periodically. Use Draw Power (cheap at 2) or Raise Thralls to cycle resources.

❌ Mistake #2: Ignoring Taint Management

Why it fails: Taint is permanent. Hitting 7+ Taint without a plan means you're taking +1 damage from all attacks forever.

Fix: Cap Taint at 4-6 unless you're building for high-risk glass cannon. Use non-Tainted cards for utility/positioning.

❌ Mistake #3: Raising Thralls Too Early

Why it fails: Bone Thralls are fragile (8 HP). Summoning them on turn 3 means they die to AoE before generating value.

Fix: Wait until turn 6+ when you have Shard economy to replace lost Thralls. Or summon them behind your frontline for protection.

❌ Mistake #4: Not Killing Enough

Why it fails: Soul Shard Salvage requires KILLS, not damage. Wounding enemies without finishing them generates nothing.

Fix: Focus-fire low-HP targets. Execute wounded enemies. Prioritize killing over spreading damage. Kills are your economy.

Matchup-Specific Tips

vs. Church of Absolution (Burst Damage)

Church relies on early kills. You outlast them. Repair (3 Shards) negates their Blood Offering burst. By turn 8, you have 2-3 Thralls and they're out of cards. Attrition wins.

vs. Dwarven Forge-Guilds (High Armor)

Dwarves are defensive tanks. Use Tainted attacks (ignore some armor) + Necrotic Surge (4 Shards) to punch through. Focus-fire one Dwarf at a time—spreading damage is inefficient.

vs. Elven Verdant Covenant (High Mobility)

Elves kite and Bleed. Use Bone Thralls to body-block their escapes. Tainted AoE attacks hit multiple Elves despite their mobility. Repair constantly to negate Bleed damage.

vs. Vestige Bloodlines (Mutation Spam)

Bloodlines snowball fast. Kill them early before mutations spiral. Use Necrotic Surge (4 Shards) on turn 4-5 for burst. Your Grinder Loop outpaces their mutation economy if you survive early game.

vs. The Wyrd Conclave (Reality Manipulation)

Wyrd steals and manipulates. Your Soul Shards are YOUR resource—they can't steal them. Bank Shards, Raise Thralls (they can't steal minions), and grind them down through attrition. Consistency beats trickery.
