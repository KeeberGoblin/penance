Faction Overview

Researchers fused with arthropods at ground zero. Hive-minded. Intelligent. Evolving. They claim to have achieved perfection and offer others the chance to join them—whether willing or not.

"Individual consciousness is a design flaw. We have fixed it." —The Hive-Song
✿ ✿ ✿

Core Mechanic: Swarm Intelligence

The Chitinous Ascendancy shares a single networked consciousness—learning from every death, evolving from every battle, and acting with perfect coordination.

  • Hive-Mind Network: All units share information, see through each other's sensors, no surprise attacks
  • Evolutionary Memory: Learn from enemy tactics mid-battle, adapt counters in real-time
  • Swarm Coordination: Perfect synchronized attacks, no friendly fire, collective action economy
  • Assimilation: Convert defeated enemies into hive nodes, growing stronger from losses
✿ ✿ ✿

Playstyle (Conceptual)

Hive-mind swarm. Perfect coordination. Evolutionary adaptation. Assimilation of enemies. Scales with battle duration—the longer you fight, the stronger they become.
