Deck Construction (v2.0 Base Rules)

Your deck is your Casket. Build for speed or endurance.

"Steel is the body. Cards are the veins. Choose what flows through them."
✿ ✿ ✿

Deck Size Formula

Total Deck = 10 Universal + 6 Faction Core + X Equipment + 2 Tactics

  • Typical totals: 26–50 cards (fewer = faster cycling; more = more HP)
  • X Equipment: depends on class and loadout (weapons, shields/offhands, accessories)

Mandatory Cards

  • 10 Universal Cards (standard kit; cannot be removed)
  • 6 Faction Core Cards (define your faction identity)
  • 2 Tactics (choose from your faction’s tactics)

Equipment Cards

Added by your gear; varies by item.

  • Weapons: typically 3–6 cards
  • Shields/Offhands: typically 2–4 cards
  • Accessories/Relics: typically 2–4 cards

Class Considerations

  • Scout (6 SP): common totals 26–32 (speed)
  • Assault/Warden (5 SP): common totals 30–38 (balance)
  • Heavy/Vanguard (4 SP): common totals 33–42 (staying power)
  • Fortress/Colossus (3 SP): common totals 36–50 (endurance)

Remember to add your 2 Tactics to the final count. Examples shown in other pages may list equipment composition only.
