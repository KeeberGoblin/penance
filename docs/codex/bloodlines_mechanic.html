The Core Mechanic: Biomass Tokens

Mutation Resource System

Generation: Gain +1 Biomass token when taking damage OR when killing an enemy. Pain and death fuel evolution.

Maximum Storage: 12 Biomass tokens

Mutation Options (Spend Biomass to activate):

  • Beast Form (3 Biomass): +3 damage to all attacks, +1 movement (lasts 2 turns)
  • Regeneration (4 Biomass): Recover 4 cards immediately
  • Feral Rage (2 Biomass): +2 damage next attack, ignore 1 Defense
  • Chitinous Armor (3 Biomass): +2 Defense (lasts 2 turns)
  • Apex Predator (6 Biomass): +5 damage, +2 movement, +1 Defense (lasts 3 turns)

How It Works

Biomass Tokens represent biological adaptation—you mutate in response to threats. The more damage you take and enemies you kill, the stronger you become. Here's the evolution cycle:

  1. Generate Biomass - Take damage (+1 per hit) OR kill enemies (+1 per kill)
  2. Bank for Mutations - Accumulate 3-6 Biomass tokens
  3. Activate Mutations - Spend Biomass to transform:
    • Beast Form (aggressive burst)
    • Regeneration (healing)
    • Feral Rage (armor-piercing spike)
    • Chitinous Armor (defensive tank mode)
    • Apex Predator (ultimate form, all bonuses)
  4. Snowball Through Combat - More fighting = more Biomass = stronger mutations = win through evolution

Example Mutation Sequence

Turn 1: Take 5 damage from enemy attack → Gain 1 Biomass (now 1 total)

Turn 2: Kill enemy → Gain 1 Biomass (now 2 total). Take 3 damage → Gain 1 Biomass (now 3 total)

Turn 3: Activate Beast Form (spend 3 Biomass) → +3 damage, +1 movement for 2 turns

Turn 4: Attack with Beast Form (+3 damage) → Kill 2 enemies → Gain 2 Biomass (now 2 total)

Turn 5: Beast Form expires. Take 4 damage → Gain 1 Biomass (now 3 total). Kill 1 enemy → Gain 1 Biomass (now 4 total)

Turn 6: Activate Regeneration (spend 4 Biomass) → Heal 4 cards back to full HP

You turned pain into power, power into kills, kills into healing. Pure biological evolution.

Companion Mechanic: Adaptive Evolution

Adaptive Evolution (Passive): When you activate a mutation, gain +1 Biomass token immediately (partial refund). Encourages aggressive mutation cycling.

Primal Instinct (Passive): When reduced to 10 HP or below, gain +2 Biomass per damage taken instead of +1. Desperate situations fuel rapid evolution.

⚖️ Bloodlines get stronger as they get hurt. Low HP = rapid Biomass generation = constant mutations = unkillable berserker.

Strategy Guide: When to Mutate

✓ Good Times to Activate Mutations

  • Beast Form (Turn 4-6) - Mid-game burst. +3 damage for 2 turns = 6+ bonus damage total. Efficient
  • Regeneration (Below 15 HP) - Don't heal at full HP. Wait until wounded, then Regenerate for maximum value
  • Feral Rage (Finishing Blow) - 2 Biomass for +2 damage + ignore 1 Defense. Perfect for killing armored enemies
  • Chitinous Armor (Before Boss Burst) - Activate when you see big damage incoming. +2 Defense for 2 turns
  • Apex Predator (Turn 8+) - Ultimate form. 6 Biomass for massive bonuses. Save for late-game dominance

✗ Bad Times to Mutate

  • Too Early (Turn 1-2) - You've only generated 1-2 Biomass. Be patient, build reserves
  • Beast Form When No Enemies - Don't activate +3 damage if there's nothing to hit. Waste of Biomass
  • Regeneration at Full HP - 4 Biomass to heal 0 damage is inefficient. Wait until wounded
  • Stacking Same Mutation - Beast Form doesn't stack with itself. Don't waste Biomass re-activating
  • Apex Predator Too Late - 6 Biomass on turn 12 when mission ends turn 13 wastes potential. Activate turn 8-10

Advanced Tactics & Combos

The Berserker Spiral

Aggressive Loop:

Take damage (gain Biomass) → Activate Beast Form (+3 damage) → Kill enemies (gain more Biomass) → Activate Feral Rage (+2 damage) → Kill more enemies → Regenerate (heal) → Repeat

Result: You turn damage taken into mutations, mutations into kills, kills into Biomass, Biomass into healing. Infinite aggression loop.

The Primal Gambit

Intentional Low HP:

Let HP drop to 9 (activate Primal Instinct: +2 Biomass per hit) → Take 3 hits (+6 Biomass) → Activate Apex Predator (6 Biomass) → Kill everything with +5 damage → Regenerate back to full HP

You weaponize being low HP, then fully heal when ready. High-risk, high-reward evolution.

The Tank Mutation

Defensive Combo:

Activate Chitinous Armor (3 Biomass, +2 Defense for 2 turns) → Tank 2-3 hits (generating Biomass while taking reduced damage) → Regenerate (4 Biomass) → Repeat

You become unkillable. +2 Defense reduces damage, Regeneration heals what gets through. Pure sustain.

The Adaptive Refund

Economy Combo:

Activate Beast Form (3 Biomass, but Adaptive Evolution refunds +1 = net 2 Biomass) → Kill enemies (+2 Biomass) → Net positive: spent 2, gained 2+ from kills

Adaptive Evolution makes mutations cheaper. You recover Biomass faster than you spend it.

Common Mistakes

❌ Mistake #1: Hoarding Biomass Without Mutating

Why it fails: Sitting at 12 Biomass cap means wasted generation. Every hit and kill beyond cap is lost value.

Fix: Spend down to 6-8 Biomass periodically. Activate Beast Form, Regenerate, or Chitinous Armor. Keep Biomass flowing.

❌ Mistake #2: Avoiding Damage

Why it fails: Biomass generation requires taking damage OR killing. Playing too defensively slows your economy.

Fix: Tank 1-2 hits early game to generate Biomass. You'll heal later with Regeneration. Embrace pain—it fuels evolution.

❌ Mistake #3: Wasting Apex Predator

Why it fails: Apex Predator costs 6 Biomass (half your cap). Using it turn 2 or turn 12 is inefficient.

Fix: Activate Apex Predator turns 8-10. You have 3 turns to dominate before mission ends. Timing maximizes value.

❌ Mistake #4: Not Using Primal Instinct

Why it fails: At 9 HP, you gain +2 Biomass per hit instead of +1. That's double generation. Playing too safe wastes this.

Fix: Intentionally drop to 9 HP mid-game. Tank 3 hits (+6 Biomass). Regenerate back to full. Weaponize low HP.

Pure Berserker

Focus: Aggressive mutations, maximum damage

Equipment: Beast Form (4 cards), Feral Rage (4 cards), Regeneration (3 cards), Light Armor (2 cards)

Strategy: Tank hits for Biomass. Activate Beast Form. Kill everything. Regenerate when low. Repeat. Unstoppable aggression.

Adaptive Tank

Focus: Defensive mutations, sustain through Regeneration

Equipment: Chitinous Armor (4 cards), Regeneration (4 cards), Beast Form (2 cards), Medium Armor (3 cards)

Strategy: Activate Chitinous Armor (+2 Defense). Tank hits with reduced damage. Regenerate constantly. Outlast through biology.

Apex Evolution

Focus: Rush to Apex Predator, dominate late-game

Equipment: Apex Predator (2 cards), Beast Form (3 cards), Regeneration (3 cards), Feral Rage (2 cards), Light Armor (2 cards)

Strategy: Bank 6 Biomass by turn 6-7. Activate Apex Predator. Destroy everything with +5 damage for 3 turns. Ultimate form wins.

Matchup-Specific Tips

vs. Church of Absolution (Burst Damage)

Church alpha strikes early. Take the damage—you gain Biomass from it. Activate Chitinous Armor (+2 Defense) to reduce follow-up hits. Regenerate when low. Their burst fuels your evolution.

vs. Dwarven Forge-Guilds (High Armor)

Dwarves have 3-4 Defense. Use Feral Rage (ignore 1 Defense) or Beast Form (+3 damage) to punch through. Your mutations bypass their static defenses. Adaptation beats stone.

vs. The Ossuarium (Attrition)

Ossuarium wants long grindy games. You excel at attrition—every hit generates Biomass, every kill generates Biomass. Regenerate constantly. Out-sustain them through mutations.

vs. Elven Verdant Covenant (Bleed Kiting)

Elves kite and Bleed. Use Beast Form (+1 movement) to chase them down. Regenerate to negate Bleed damage. Your healing outpaces their DoT. Aggression beats kiting.

vs. The Exchange (Economic Warfare)

Exchange hires Mercenaries. Kill their mercs for Biomass (+1 per kill). Tank damage from them for Biomass (+1 per hit). They fund your evolution. Their economy fuels yours.
