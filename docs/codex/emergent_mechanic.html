The Core Mechanic: Metamorph Tokens & Hive Coordination

Transformation Resource System

Swarm Intelligence (Passive): Gain +2 Metamorph tokens at start of each round (+4 if 3+ Emergent allies within 4 hexes). Proximity breeds power.

Maximum Storage: 15 Metamorph tokens

Transformation Forms (Spend Metamorph to activate):

  • Assault Form (2 Metamorph): +3 damage, -1 Defense (lasts 2 turns)
  • Tank Form (2 Metamorph): +4 Defense, -1 movement (lasts 2 turns)
  • Scout Form (2 Metamorph): +3 movement, ignore terrain (lasts 2 turns)
  • Hive-Mind Link (1 Metamorph): Copy ally's card at 75% effectiveness
  • Chrysalis Rebirth (5 Metamorph): Cannot act 1 turn. Next turn: recover 10 cards, remove Heat, +2 Defense permanently (once per mission)

⚖️ Balance Update Oct 19: Doubled from +1/+2 to +2/+4 Metamorph generation. Buffed all forms. Increased Hive-Mind Link from 50% to 75% effectiveness.

How It Works

Metamorph Tokens represent the Emergent hive's collective evolution. The more allies you have nearby, the faster you generate tokens. Transform into specialized forms or link minds with allies for powerful synergies. Here's the swarm cycle:

  1. Generate Metamorph Tokens - +2 per round solo, +4 per round if 3+ Emergent allies within 4 hexes
  2. Stay Close to Allies - Proximity is power. 4-hex range is generous—maintain formation
  3. Transform Strategically - Activate forms based on situation:
    • Assault Form (aggressive push)
    • Tank Form (defend allies)
    • Scout Form (mobility/flanking)
    • Hive-Mind Link (copy powerful ally cards)
  4. Chrysalis Rebirth (Ultimate) - Once per mission, spend 5 Metamorph to go dormant 1 turn, then fully heal + permanent defense buff

Example Hive Coordination

Turn 1: Start with 3 Emergent allies within 4 hexes → Gain +4 Metamorph (now 4 total)

Turn 2: Gain +4 Metamorph (now 8 total). Activate Assault Form (2 Metamorph) → +3 damage for 2 turns

Turn 3: Assault Form active. Ally plays powerful attack → Spend 1 Metamorph for Hive-Mind Link (copy at 75% effectiveness)

Turn 4: Assault Form expires. Gain +4 Metamorph (now 9 total). Activate Tank Form (2 Metamorph) → +4 Defense for 2 turns

Turns 5-6: Tank Form absorbs damage. Continue generating +4 Metamorph/turn. Bank for Chrysalis Rebirth

Turn 7: At 8 HP with 12 Metamorph. Activate Chrysalis Rebirth (5 Metamorph) → Dormant for 1 turn

Turn 8: Rebirth completes → Recover 10 cards (back to 18 HP), remove Heat, gain +2 Defense permanently

The hive never dies. We adapt, we transform, we endure.

Companion Mechanic: Pheromone Command

Pheromone Command (Active, 3 Metamorph): Release pheromone cloud. All Emergent allies within 5 hexes gain +1 damage and +1 SP. All enemies in range have -1 to hit (lasts 2 turns).

Exoskeleton Molt (Active, 1 Metamorph): When damaged, reduce damage by 3 and recover 2 cards (shedding damaged shell). Once per round.

⚖️ Pheromone Command is team-wide buff. Exoskeleton Molt is emergency defense. Emergent excel in team play.

Strategy Guide: When to Transform

✓ Good Times to Use Metamorph Forms

  • Assault Form (Aggressive Push) - When you need burst damage. +3 damage for 2 turns = 6+ bonus damage total
  • Tank Form (Protecting Allies) - Before enemy burst. +4 Defense for 2 turns makes you unkillable bodyguard
  • Scout Form (Flanking/Objectives) - Need to reach distant objectives quickly. +3 movement, ignore terrain
  • Hive-Mind Link (Ally Has Powerful Card) - Ally plays 8-damage attack? Copy it at 75% (6 damage) for only 1 Metamorph. Insane value
  • Chrysalis Rebirth (Desperate Situation) - Below 10 HP with no healing? Chrysalis for full recovery + permanent buff

✗ Bad Times to Transform

  • Assault Form When Defensive - -1 Defense makes you fragile. Don't activate when expecting enemy burst
  • Tank Form When Chasing - -1 movement makes kiting impossible. Don't activate against mobile enemies
  • Scout Form in Melee - No combat bonuses. Waste of Metamorph if not repositioning
  • Hive-Mind Link on Weak Cards - Don't copy a 2-damage attack. Save for powerful cards (6+ damage or game-changing effects)
  • Chrysalis Too Late - Turn 12 Chrysalis means you're dormant turn 12, rebirth turn 13, mission ends turn 14. Too late

Common Mistakes

❌ Mistake #1: Playing Solo

Why it fails: Solo generation is +2/turn. With 3+ allies nearby, it's +4/turn. You're halving your economy by staying isolated.

Fix: Stay within 4 hexes of allies. 4 hexes is generous—maintain hive formation. Proximity is power.

❌ Mistake #2: Hoarding Metamorph Without Transforming

Why it fails: Sitting at 15 Metamorph cap wastes generation. Every round beyond cap is lost value.

Fix: Transform frequently. Spend 2 Metamorph on Assault/Tank/Scout Forms. Use Hive-Mind Link (1 Metamorph) to cycle resources.

❌ Mistake #3: Not Using Hive-Mind Link

Why it fails: Hive-Mind Link costs 1 Metamorph to copy an ally's card at 75%. That's insane value—you're getting an 8-damage card for 1 token.

Fix: Always watch for powerful ally cards. Copy them with Hive-Mind Link. Amplify your team's best plays.

❌ Mistake #4: Wasting Chrysalis Rebirth

Why it fails: Chrysalis at 25 HP is inefficient. You heal 10 cards but you're only missing 5. Wasted healing.

Fix: Use Chrysalis when below 10 HP. Maximize the 10-card recovery. Also removes Heat—use when Heat is high.

Matchup-Specific Tips

vs. Church of Absolution (Burst Damage)

Church alpha strikes early. Activate Tank Form (+4 Defense) on turn 2-3 to negate their Blood Offering burst. Use Exoskeleton Molt to reduce damage further. Defense beats burst.

vs. Dwarven Forge-Guilds (High Armor)

Dwarves are defensive tanks. Activate Assault Form (+3 damage) and use Hive-Mind Link to copy ally attacks. Swarm them with multiple +3 damage hits. Numbers beat armor.

vs. The Ossuarium (Attrition)

Ossuarium wants long grindy games. You excel at sustain—Chrysalis Rebirth heals 10 cards, Exoskeleton Molt recovers 2 cards per use. Out-sustain them through transformations.

vs. Elven Verdant Covenant (Bleed Kiting)

Elves kite and Bleed. Use Scout Form (+3 movement) to chase them down. Tank Form (+4 Defense) reduces Bleed damage. Hive-Mind Link to copy ally attacks while chasing.

vs. The Exchange (Economic Warfare)

Exchange hires Mercenaries. Stay grouped with allies (+4 Metamorph/turn). Swarm their mercs with coordinated Assault Forms. Pheromone Command buffs your team while debuffing theirs. Coordination beats mercenaries.
