Support Units as Main Characters (Optional Mode)

Play a support unit instead of a Casket for an underdog challenge.

"When the giants fall, the small must carry the fire."
✿ ✿ ✿

Core Changes

  • HP: ×4 the unit’s normal HP (or use scenario-specified totals)
  • SP: 5 per turn (unless unit card states otherwise)
  • Deck: 20-card unit deck, or use unit reference where provided
  • Actions: Follow support unit rules; some Casket-only effects are disabled

Restrictions

  • No Casket-only equipment or tactics
  • Cannot trigger Casket Component Damage effects
  • Scenario special rules may override these defaults

This mode is ideal for narrative play or asymmetric missions. Calibrate enemy strength accordingly.
