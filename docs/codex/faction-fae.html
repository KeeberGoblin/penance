Faction Overview

The fae do not declare war. They simply appear, and settlements die. Tricksters, oath-keepers, and reality-benders who find the post-apocalypse endlessly amusing.

"The world ended? How delightful. Now we can truly play." —Fae Philosophy
✿ ✿ ✿

Core Mechanic: Wyrd Tokens

The Wyrd Conclave manipulates reality through Wyrd Tokens—reality distortions that bend space, steal faces, and make the impossible merely improbable.

  • Reality Distortion: Teleport across the battlefield, phase through obstacles, rewrite combat outcomes
  • Stolen Faces: Impersonate enemies, sow confusion, turn allies against each other
  • Oath-Binding: Force enemies into supernatural contracts with devastating penalties
  • Trickery: Every action has hidden costs, every deal has fine print written in blood
✿ ✿ ✿

Playstyle (Conceptual)

Reality-bending control. Teleportation. Illusions. Oath-bound contracts. Every mechanic is a trick, every victory feels unfair. High skill ceiling, deeply punishing to master.
