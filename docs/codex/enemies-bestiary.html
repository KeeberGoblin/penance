Enemy Categories

Swarm (3–5 HP): Weak enemies deployed in large numbers (5–10 per mission). Simple AI. Elite (15–25 HP): 1–2 per mission. Medium complexity AI. Mini-bosses. Boss (30–60 HP): Mission capstone. Complex AI with phases. Anomalous: Reality-defying entities (see Anomalous Bestiary).

▼ Boss Enemies (30–60 HP) — Mission Capstone

Epic encounters. Complex AI with multiple phases. Every battle is a campaign moment. Bosses carry legendary loot and define the tone of your mission.

9. Tainted Leviathan
Boss (Monster)
HP: 40 | MOVE: 4 | ARMOR: 5 | SP: 5
Phases: Phase 1 (40–25 HP) Basic attacks. Phase 2 (24–10 HP) Enraged (+2 dmg, +1 Move). Phase 3 (9–0 HP) Berserk (AOE, regeneration). AI: Crushing Bite (×10, 5d6 + knockback) + Tail Sweep (×6, AOE) + Charge (×5) + Regenerate (×4). Massive (4 hexes). Armored Hide (reduce damage by 5). Loot: 500 Credits, 8d6 Scrap, Leviathan Fang (Legendary).
10. Rogue Casket (Enemy Pilot)
Boss (Rival Pilot)
HP: 30 | MOVE: 5 | ARMOR: 2 | SP: 5
Mechanics: Full player rules. 30-card deck, SP economy, Component Damage, Heat management. AI Priority: Below 10 HP → defensive. In melee → attack. 3–6 hexes → move/ranged. 7+ hexes → sprint. Fights like player. Manages SP, uses cover, flanks, retreats. Loot: 300 Credits, 4d6 Scrap, entire enemy deck (30 cards), Legendary relic.
11. Bonelord Thresh (Ossuarium Boss)
Boss (Faction Champion)
HP: 50 | MOVE: 4 | ARMOR: 3 | SP: 5
Phases: Phase 1 (50–35 HP) Summons undead. Phase 2 (34–15 HP) Lifesteal attacks. Phase 3 (14–0 HP) Resurrection + AOE corruption. AI Phase 1 (50 cards): Soul Harvest (×12, lifesteal on kill) + Raise Dead (×8, summon Bone Thralls) + Bone Armor (×6) + Necrotic Bolt (×5). Phase 2 adds: Vampiric Strike (×4) + Mass Raise Dead (×2). Phase 3 adds: Resurrection (×1, once per battle) + Apocalyptic Harvest (×3, 5d6 + 3 Taint AOE). Necromantic Aura (auto-summon Thralls from corpses). The Ledger (+1 dmg per kill). Loot: 600 Credits, 10d6 Scrap, The Ledger (Legendary cursed relic).
