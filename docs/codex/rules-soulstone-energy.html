Overview

Every Casket is powered by a Soulstone Core containing a bound soul. This soul provides bioelectric energy to drive hydraulics, servos, and weapons. Energy depletes during missions and must be restored through Soulstone refinement.

Important: The Soulstone Core soul is not the pilot. It's a separate trapped consciousness powering the machine. The living pilot operates the casket while the dead soul fuels it.

Quick Reference

  • Max Energy: 100 (full Soulstone Core)
  • Starting Energy: 80 (most caskets)
  • Depletion Rate: -2 to -15 per mission
  • Restoration: Refine Soulstone Shards between missions
  • At 0 Energy: Casket inoperable (cannot deploy)

Energy Restoration (Between Missions)

Soulstone Refinement Process

To restore Energy, pilots must refine Soulstone Shards into their Casket's Core. This process crushes smaller crystals (containing soul fragments) and feeds them to the Core soul.

Shard Tier Energy Restored Taint Gained Time Required
T1 Shard +5 Energy +1 Taint 1 day
T2 Fragment +15 Energy +2 Taint 2 days
T3 Crystal +40 Energy +5 Taint 5 days
T4 Core (Replacement) +100 Energy +10 Taint 2 weeks (requires soul-binding ritual)

Refinement Examples

Example 1: Light Restoration
Casket at 64/100 Energy. Refine 3x T1 Shards.
Result: +15 Energy (79/100), +3 Taint, 3 days downtime

Example 2: Heavy Restoration
Casket at 15/100 Energy (Critical). Refine 1x T3 Crystal + 5x T1 Shards.
Result: +65 Energy (80/100), +10 Taint, 10 days downtime

Warning: Refinement always gains Taint (Void corruption). High Taint = instability, random events, casket going rogue. Manage Taint carefully!

Emergency Field Recharge (Mid-Mission)

Once per mission, a pilot can perform an emergency recharge by manually crushing a Shard inside the casket.

Procedure

  1. Requires 1 full turn (no movement, no attacks, vulnerable)
  2. Pilot must have at least 1 Shard in inventory
  3. Pilot manually crushes Shard against Soulstone Core socket
  4. Core absorbs essence immediately (screams audibly through radio)

Risk Table (Roll 1d6)

Roll Result Effect
1-3 Success Restore Energy as normal, gain Taint as normal
4-5 Partial Overload Restore Energy, +2x Taint (double corruption)
6 Critical Overload Restore Energy, +3x Taint, immediate Thread Snap (random thread severs)

Narrative: Crystal fragments pierce pilot's hands. Blood mixes with soul essence. Core soul screams so loudly other units hear it through closed caskets. Pilot's neural threads burn from Core overload.

Core Burnout (End of Lifespan)

Soulstone Cores do not last forever. After 500 total Energy cycled (approximately 20-50 missions), the Core begins terminal degradation.

Burnout Warning Signs

Total Energy Cycled Symptoms Effects
0-400 Healthy Normal operation
401-450 Aging Energy restoration -10% efficiency
451-480 Declining Energy restoration -20% efficiency, +1 Taint per refinement
481-495 Failing Energy restoration -30% efficiency, +2 Taint per refinement, visible cracks
496-500 Critical Core pulses erratically, imminent failure
500+ Burnout Core shatters, casket loses all power, pilot must be extracted immediately

Replacement Cost: New T4 Core = 1,000-1,500 Credits (requires fresh soul-binding ritual). Salvage from burned Core = 5-10 T1 Shards (grim recycling).

Campaign Integration

Energy Management Tips

  • Track Energy on character sheet: Mark current/max (e.g., 64/100)
  • Plan refinement during downtime: Budget Shards for long campaigns
  • Monitor Taint accumulation: Balance Energy restoration vs. corruption risk
  • Save T3 Crystals for emergencies: Big Energy boost when critically low
  • Watch for burnout signs: Replace Core before it fails mid-mission

GM Guidelines

  • Award Shards as loot: Use mission reward table (Standard = 1-2 T1, Boss = 5-8 T1 + 2-3 T2 + 1 T3)
  • Make refinement scenes meaningful: Describe Core soul screaming, faces appearing in crystal
  • Use Energy depletion as tension: Low Energy = harder missions, force tough choices
  • Track total Energy cycled: Warn players when approaching 400+ (Core aging)
  • Core burnout = campaign event: Make it dramatic (crystal shatters mid-battle, pilot must be rescued)
