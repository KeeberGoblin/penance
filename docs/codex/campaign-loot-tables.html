"Fortune favors the bold. The dead favor no one."

Loot Design Philosophy

Loot is scarce (forces choices), thematic (reflects where/what you killed), random (dice determine fate), and impactful (rare loot can change campaigns). This system emphasizes risk/reward: higher difficulty missions yield better rewards, but come with greater danger.

Common Loot

Repair Kit (Basic)

50 Credits

Effect: Remove 1 Damage card from deck (settlement phase). Essential for field repairs.

Rarity: Most missions yield common loot.

Uncommon Loot

T2 Soulstone Fragment

75 Credits

Effect: Fist-sized fragment. +15 Energy when refined (+2 Taint). Useful for mid-tier crafting.

Rarity: Hidden objectives and elite enemies drop these.

Rare Loot

Void Crystal

400 Credits

Effect: Craft into weapon: attacks deal +2 damage, gain +1 Taint per kill. Power at a cost.

Rarity: Boss encounters and special missions.

Legendary Loot

Theslar's Locket

1000 Credits

Effect: Contains Theslar's voice (AI assistant). Grants Probability Matrix ability. +1 Taint per mission.

Rarity: Campaign-defining. Only 1–2 per full campaign.
