PENANCE: Card Anatomy

Comprehensive breakdown of Penance card design elements

Card Component Details

1. SP Cost: Strategy Points required to play this card. Ranges from 0-5. Free actions (0 SP) are powerful but limited.
2. Card Name: Unique identifier. Often thematic (e.g., "Faithful Thrust" for Church faction, emphasizing religious devotion).
3. Card Type: Primary category...Attack, Defense, Movement, Utility, Gambit (risky high-reward), or Reactive (interrupt).
4. Stats Bar: Quick-reference for Range (Melee/Ranged 1-6), Damage output, and Heat generation (+0 to +3).
5. Effect Text: Complete mechanical description. Uses keywords like "discard," "ignore Defense," "self-harm" for consistency.
6. Keywords: Searchable tags for deck building. Examples: MELEE, SELF-HARM, PIERCE, BLEED, REACTIVE.
7. Flavor Text: Italicized lore. Sets tone without affecting gameplay. Church cards emphasize suffering/redemption.
8. Footer: Faction name + card number in set (3/21 = 3rd card of 21 in this faction).
9. Border: Gold standard border. Potential variants: Silver (rare), Red (legendary), or faction-specific colors.
