WHY PLAY PENANCE?

BRUTAL TACTICAL COMBAT

Deck-as-HP system. Component destruction. Death spirals. Kingdom Death: Monster-style brutality meets GKR: Heavy Hitters elegance.

ASYMMETRIC FACTIONS

Church martyrs who self-harm for power. Dwarven engineers with rune counters. Undead necromancers. 9 unique playstyles.

BODY HORROR MECHS

Pilot your own corpse in a Casket war machine. Sacrifice limbs for power. Permanent consequences. The Puppeteer Interface connects your threads to dead flesh.

437 YEARS OF LORE

Post-apocalyptic world built on the ruins of a failed immortality experiment. Deep narrative campaign with settlements, pilot scars, and faction politics.

437 YEARS OF HISTORY

The recorded history of a world that ended. What remains are fragments, transmissions, and the testimony of those who survived.

YEAR 0

The Sundering

The Resonance Engine activates. The world ends in 437 seconds. Dr. Nikolas Theslar's final transmission: "It's singing. God help us."

YEAR 3

First Casket

The first Remnant builds herself a body from scrap and corpses. Post-mortem consciousness persistence becomes "The Curse."

YEAR 47

Church Founded

The Church of Absolution declares redemption through violence. "We will earn our rest, or walk this world forever."

YEAR 134

The Great Schism

The Church splits over who deserves salvation. The Covenant of Broken Chains forms. Civil war lasts 80 years.

YEAR 223

Betrayal at Roothold

The Church burns half the elven forests. The Church-Elf war begins. 214 years later, it has not ended.

YEAR 437

Present Day

Nine factions war across a shattered world. The dead still walk. The Engine still sings. No absolution has come.

KEY MECHANICS

Deck-as-HP
30 cards = 30 HP. Discard cards when damaged. Reshuffle adds Damage cards (death spiral).
Component Destruction
3 Component Damage = Destroyed. Lose entire card type. Kingdom Death-style brutality.
SP Economy
Play multiple cards per turn. Safe Zone vs Danger Zone. Heat Strain checks at 5+ Heat.
Faction Asymmetry
Church: Self-harm for burst damage. Dwarves: Armor-piercing + Rune Counters. Completely different playstyles.
Facing Modifiers
Rear arc +2 damage. Positioning matters. Flank for advantage.
Reactive Cards
0 SP interrupts. Play during opponent's turn. Brace for Impact, Unbreakable.
