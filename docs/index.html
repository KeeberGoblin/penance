WHY PLAY PENANCE?

BRUTAL TACTICAL COMBAT

Deck-as-HP system. Component destruction. Death spirals. Kingdom Death: Monster-style brutality meets GKR: Heavy Hitters elegance.

CUSTOM DICE SYSTEM

3 custom d6 dice with symbols. GKR-style to-hit rolls with BattleTech modifiers. Defense Dice create brutal variance. Every attack is a risk for attacker and defender.

MODULAR EQUIPMENT SYSTEM

60+ weapons, shields, plating, and sigils. Variable deck sizes (26-50 cards). Craft, loot, and smelt. Every loadout tells a story.

ASYMMETRIC FACTIONS

Church martyrs self-harm for power. Dwarves stack armor. Undead steal life. Elves bleed enemies. 4 unique playstyles, more coming.

CAMPAIGN PROGRESSION

KDM-style event tables (132 events). Permanent pilot scars. Settlement building. Anomalous artifacts. NPC encounters. Every campaign is unique.

437 YEARS OF HISTORY

The recorded history of a world that ended. What remains are fragments, transmissions, and the testimony of those who survived.

YEAR 0

The Sundering

The Resonance Engine activates. Reality tears. Dr. Nikolas Theslar's final transmission: "It's singing. God help us, it's singing."

YEAR 52

First Casket

Engineer Gareth volunteers for the prototype. Six minutes of screaming. When they open the Casket, he won't leave. "I feel hollow without it."

YEAR 78

The Ledger Opens

Bonelord Thresh signs his first soul contract. "Resurrection for your son. Your soul when you die. Read the fine print." She signs.

YEAR 134

The Great Schism

The Church splits over who deserves salvation. The Covenant of Broken Chains forms. Civil war lasts 80 years. No one wins.

YEAR 223

Betrayal at Roothold

The Church burns half the elven forests. "They sheltered the Tainted." The Church-Elf war begins. 214 years later, it has not ended.

YEAR 437

Present Day

Nine factions war across a shattered world. The dead still walk. The Engine still sings. No absolution has come.

FACTIONS

Four playable factions, each with unique mechanics and asymmetric gameplay. Nine factions exist in the lore, warring for control of a dying world.

CHURCH OF ABSOLUTION

Playstyle

Aggressive martyrdom. Self-harm for burst damage. Ally protection through sacrifice. Scales with kills.

Signature Mechanic

Blood Offering: Discard 2 cards (self-harm) for +3 damage. Martyrdom Protocol: Redirect damage from allies to yourself. Righteous Fury: +1 damage per enemy killed (permanent).

Status

✅ PLAYABLE - Complete deck with 4 sample builds

DWARVEN FORGE-GUILDS

Playstyle

Defensive attrition tank. Stack rune counters for damage reduction. All attacks armor-piercing. Convert Heat to damage.

Signature Mechanic

Rune of Protection: Stack up to 3 Rune Counters (-3 damage per hit). Stone Endurance: 32 HP instead of 30. Clan Vengeance: +2 damage per Component Damage taken.

Status

✅ PLAYABLE - Complete deck with 4 sample builds

THE OSSUARIUM

Playstyle

Lifesteal vampire. Drain HP from enemies to recover cards. Triple resurrection mechanics. Summon undead minions.

Signature Mechanic

Soul Harvest: Deal 4 damage, recover 4 cards. Corpse Fuel: Recover 5 cards when enemy dies. Phylactery: Automatically resurrect at 5 HP (once per mission). Decay cards instead of Damage cards on reshuffle.

Status

✅ PLAYABLE - Complete deck with 4 sample builds

ELVEN VERDANT COVENANT

Playstyle

Hit-and-run assassin. Stack Bleed damage-over-time (infinite stacking). High mobility with teleports. Passive regeneration (3 cards per round).

Signature Mechanic

Bleed Stacking: Enemies take damage at start of their turn (stacks infinitely). Leaf Dance: Move 3 hexes + attack + continue moving. Photosynthesis + Verdant Regeneration: Recover 2 cards per round (passive).

Status

✅ PLAYABLE - Complete deck with 4 sample builds
