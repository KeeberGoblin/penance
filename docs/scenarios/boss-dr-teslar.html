The Legend

Dr. Teslar is not dead. He never was. When the Theslar Engine's core breached 437 years ago, it consumed him—body, mind, and soul—and spat out something else. Something that whispers from the Wound. Something that knows your name before you speak it.

He appears as a translucent figure encased in crackling Taint energy, half-corporeal, half-nightmare. His Casket is not a machine but an extension of the Engine itself—a writhing mass of cables, flesh, and geometry that should not exist.

He does not fight to kill you. He fights to understand you. To break you apart and study the pieces. To add you to the Engine's eternal song.

Mutating HP Deck System

Unlike Sister Vex (who adds cards), Dr. Teslar's AI deck MUTATES. When he reshuffles at phase thresholds, ALL remaining cards transform into more powerful versions. His strategy evolves organically as the fight progresses.

How It Works:

  1. Damage = Discard: When Dr. Teslar takes 6 damage, discard 6 cards from his HP deck.
  2. Boss Turn: After ALL players act, Dr. Teslar draws 4 cards (5 in Phase 4) and plays them all.
  3. Mutation Triggers: When he reshuffles at 34, 19, or 7 cards remaining, ALL cards transform.
  4. Example: "Theorem of Pain" (4d6 attack) → "Axiom of Agony" (6d6, ignore Defense) → "Certainty of Damnation" (8d6, mind control)
  5. Victory: Boss defeated when deck AND hand empty, Instability <20, and cannot reshuffle.

Setup Instructions

Boss Starting Position:

Place Dr. Teslar in the center of a 16×16 hex grid labeled "The Engine Core Chamber."

Boss Deck Construction:

Shuffle the following 50 cards to create Dr. Teslar's starting HP deck:

  • 15× "Theorem of Pain" (Basic Attack)
  • 6× "Phase Shift" (Teleport)
  • 4× "Dimensional Anchor" (Control)
  • 10× "Taint Cascade" (AOE Corruption)
  • 5× "Reality Distortion" (Terrain Manipulation)
  • 5× "Hypothesis of Suffering" (Debuff)
  • 5× "The Wound Gazes Back" (Willpower Check)

Player Setup:

  • 3-4 Casket pilots (2 = too easy, 5+ = impossible)
  • Standard 30-card decks (veteran campaign decks recommended)
  • Place players 8-10 hexes from boss (edges of arena)
  • Each player starts with 3 Soulstone Fragments (consumable: remove 3 Taint)
  • Track Instability on paper (starts at 0)

Special Terrain (Engine Core Chamber):

  • Engine Core (center, 4 hexes): Impassable. Enemies adjacent gain +1 Taint per turn.
  • Void Rifts (4+ hexes, spawn at Instability 10+): Entering costs 5 SP and deals 3d6 damage. Enemies inside cannot be targeted by ranged attacks.
  • Taint Pools (6 hexes, blue glowing): Difficult terrain. Remove 2 Heat at end of turn, but gain 1 Taint.
  • Stabilization Pillars (4 corners): Activating (3 SP, must be adjacent) reduces Instability by 2. Can only be used once each.

Boss Turn Sequence:

  1. After ALL players have taken their turns, Dr. Teslar activates
  2. Draw 4 cards from HP deck (5 cards in Phase 4)
  3. Play all 4-5 cards in order drawn (resolve effects)
  4. Discard cards to separate discard pile
  5. If deck is empty, reshuffle immediately and ALL cards transform based on phase
  6. Check Instability Track and apply reality effects
